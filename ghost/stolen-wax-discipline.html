Compendium Ghost Shifting Ghost-Clay Path

Stolen Wax Discipline

Costs: 5m, 1lhl, Mins: Compassion 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Moliation, Obvious, Shaping, Touch
Duration: One hour per dot of Essence
Prerequisite charms: Waxen Ghostly Flesh
Source: The Books of Sorcery, Vol. IV — the Roll of Glorious Divinity II (p. 145)

This Charm allows a masquer to apply the effects of Waxen Ghostly Flesh to another ghost’s body. The target ghost must be willing to be transformed, and the artist must mold and reshape the target’s body physically, like a potter working wet clay. The roll for, difficulty of and possible scope of alterations are the same as for Waxen Ghostly Flesh. All changes last for one hour per dot of Essence the masquer has and cannot be extended by spending Willpower points. Extending the duration requires the target to use Steeling the Spirit or the artist to use Prison of the New Form.
