Compendium Ghost Tenacious Merchant’s Way

Careful Debtor Stance

Costs: 1wp, Mins: Conviction 2, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Touch
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Earnest Creditor Technique
Source: The Books of Sorcery, Vol. IV — the Roll of Glorious Divinity II (p. 154)

This Charm allows a ghost to take motes of Essence from a willing party with motes to spend. The ghost need only touch his creditor, and the creditor must be willing to allow the ghost to withdraw Essence. The ghost then spends one Willpower point as the player rolls (Conviction + Bureaucracy). For every success on the roll, the ghost may take one mote of Essence.

This Charm fails if the prospective creditor is unwilling to transfer Essence.
