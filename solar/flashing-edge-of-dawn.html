Flashing Edge of Dawn

Costs: —, Mins: Melee 4, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: One Weapon, Two Blows
Source: Glories of the Most High — the Unconquered Sun (p. 22), Scroll of Errata (p. 184)

This Charm permanently enhances its prerequisite. If she wishes to, the Solar may ignore steps 7-9 on her second damage application and simply repeat step 10. Additionally, her post-soak damage is raised by one die whenever she uses One Weapon, Two Blows.
