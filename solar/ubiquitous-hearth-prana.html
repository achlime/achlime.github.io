Compendium Solar Occult

Ubiquitous Hearth Prana

Costs: 6m, 2wp, Mins: Occult 4, Essence 3, Type: Simple
Keywords: Obvious, Shaping, Combo-OK, Residence
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Any Occult Excellency
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

Regardless of the challenges they face, the Chosen of the Sun will always return home. Neither desolate wastelands, nor the deepest oceans, nor the thickest jungles will prevent them from coming home. After a short ritual befitting the character of the Solar, she disappears from her current location and appears at the entrance of her residence.

When activated, this Charm relocates the Solar to her residence as a Shaping effect. If she leaves the residence, she will reappear at the location she left from. If this Charm is ended for any reason, which includes cancelling the motes comitted to this Charm, she will also reappear at the location she left from. Her presence in the manse is a shaping effect.
