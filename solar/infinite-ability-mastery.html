Infinite (Ability) Mastery

Costs: 2m+, Mins: (Ability) 4, Essence 3, Type: Simple
Keywords: Mirror (Infinite (Ability) Mastery)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Any (Ability) Excellency
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 185-187), Scroll of Errata (p. 20)

The Solar Exalted are infinite wells of talent. Each two motes committed to this Charm reduces the mote cost for the first (Ability) Excellencies by one, to a minimum of 0. Exalted with Essence 3 can spend up to six motes on this Charm, while Exalted with Essence 4+ can spend as many motes as they like.

Example: A character with Infinite Melee Mastery, a Melee 4, a Dexterity of 4 and an Essence of 3 could commit up to six motes to the Charm. This would reduce the cost of any given Melee Excellency by three motes. While the character is technically using a Charm fo the purposes of Combos, she could gain up to three bonus dice from the First Excellency on any action using Melee at a cost 0 motes. She could buy one success with the Second Excellency at not cost in motes or reduce the cost of the Third Excellency to one mote.

She could also gain one die with the First Excellency if the First and Second Excellencies were together in a Combo she had activated (see p. 244). Any additional effect—adding more dice or more success—would cost the normal price. So, the example chraacter could purchase up to eight bonus dice using the First Excellency and apply a three-mote discount to the total cost, so buying the maximum of eight dice would cost five motes.

Apply the discount to the total expenditure on Excellencies relating to a given roll. A character who spends motes on the Comboed effects of the First and Second Excellency does not receive the discount for the use of both Excellencies on a given roll. If the example character activated a Combo and spent fout motes to buy to successes on a roll with the Second Excellency and four motes to buy four dice using the First Excellency, the cost would be five motes ([4 + 4] - [3 for the six motes committed to the Infinite Melee Master]).

This Charm and the relevant discount are incompatible with any effect that invokes the effects of Excellencies as innate powers rather than as Charms. A character must have the Excellency in order to invoke it at a discount through the use of this Charm.

The discount of this Charm does not apply to dice purchased over the Solar’s normal dice limit with (Ability) Essence Flow.
