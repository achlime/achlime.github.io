Compendium Solar War

Heroism-Encouraging Presence

Costs: 5m or 5m, 1wp, Mins: War 4, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, War
Duration: One Scene
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 197-198), Scroll of Errata (p. 25)

The Chose radiate the confidence and surety of the Unconquered Sun. This Charm affects any unit or social group that the Solar commands and every ally within (Essence × 20) yards. This Charm’s targets no longer need to make Valor checks. In war, this Charm prevents rout and reduces the chance of fatigue (see p. 374).

This Charm costs five motes when used in combat or social combat, or five motes, one Willpower when used in mass combat.

At Essence 3+, in addition to the listed effects, whenever the Solar or an ally under the effects of Heroism-Encouraging Presence channels Valor to enhance an action, they add successes rather than dice to the action at a rate of one success for every die that would have been added. If the actions succeeds, the character is automatically refunded the Willpower spent to channel Valor.
