Compendium Solar Martial Arts

Thunder Breaks the Clouds

Costs: 10m, 2wp, Mins: Martial Arts 4, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive (Step 10)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Solar Hero Form
Source: Under the Rose (p. 30)

The character’s fists blaze with the revelatory truth of the Unconquered Sun. This Charm may be activated whenever the Solar successfully strikes a being with an unarmed attack. Tongues of cleansing golden flames erupt from the target’s eyes and mouth and she screams in pain as every instance of unnatural mental influence affecting her abruptly terminates regardless of source or potency. Only the fealty of akuma, the bindings of demons, the Great Curse and similarly inviolable effects chosen by the Storyteller ignore this Charm.
