Compendium Solar Awareness

Eye of the Unconquered Sun

Costs: 12m, 1p, Mins: Awareness 5, Essence 5, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Unsurpassed Sight Discipline
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 226)

The Lawgivers see through all deceptions. This Charm lets the Solar see everything that is deliberately concealed within range of his vision. This Charm renders Stealth effects—natural and unnatural—inapplicable against the Solar. She may notice the effects, but they have not effect on her. This includes invisibility, mundane disguises, disguises augmented by Lunar shapeshifting or Sidereal astrology and Compulsion and Illusion effects that force her to deny reality. This Charm flares the Solar’s anima to the 16+ motes leve while in use. If another Charm contests this Charm’s effects, Eye of the Unconquered Sun adds twice the Solar’s Essence in automatic successes to the opposed roll to maintain the effects of this Charm. This charm is considered a defense when used to prevent a surprise or social attack.
