Compendium Solar Sail

Sun-Racing Clip

Costs: 10m, 1wp, Mins: Sail 4, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Screaming Wind Voyage)
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Perfect Reckoning Technique
Source: Hundredfold Facets of Enlightenment (p. 30), Custom (Brend Wanders)

The glorious vitality of a life at sea fills not just the crew, but the vessel itself. To activate this Charm, the Solar must be on board and authorized to navigate the ship. Once activated, the Charm affects the ship indefinitely while the Solar is aboard. The Charm ends if, for any reason, the Solar is no longer authorized to navigate the ship. While the Exalt is aboard the vessel, its maximum speed increases by (Essence x 5) miles per day.

New Abbyssal Charm: Screaming Wind Voyage (Prerequisites: Dark Water Odyssey). This Charm is identical to its Solar counterpart.
