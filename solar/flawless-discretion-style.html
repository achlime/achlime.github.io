Compendium Solar Socialize

Flawless Discretion Style

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Socialize 4, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Illusion, Social
Duration: One Scene
Prerequisite charms: Any Socialize Excellency, Mastery of Small Manners
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

The relations of the Lawgivers are theirs to know, and theirs only. Flawless Discretion Style masks the Solar’s social interaction from those who might observe the character. The Exalt’s player rolls (Manipulation + Socialize), adding her Essence in automatic successes. This Charm exerts unnatural mental influence on all those who observe her. if the character’s sucesses exceed a target’s Dodge MDV, the target is fooled by the illusion and can not make out any details with regards to the social interaction. The target must spend four Willpower to resist the illusion, which drops to one Willpower if someone calls the target’s attention to the details of the Solar’s interaction.

This Charm’s illusion prevents observers from determining the exact nature of the social interaction, and covers the entire social interaction with all participants. Observers can still determine who was involved, but can no longer determine what is being discussed or done. They can only observe the social interaction in generic terms: a long discussion about how to break open the king’s vault becomes “they talked”, even if the observer was standing next to the Exalt; an illicit encounter between the queen and her suitor becomes “they met”, even if this was in the throne room under the eyes of the king himself; the under-the-counter buying of a vial of posion from a herbalist becomes “they traded”, even if the guard is expressly watching the exalt the whole transaction. But pouring the poison in the kings wine, or picking the lock on the vault can not be masked as this is not a social interaction.
