Compendium Solar Occult

Eternal Hospitality Grant

Costs: 12m, 1wp, Mins: Occult 5, Essence 4, Type: Simple
Keywords: Obvious, Shaping, Training, Combo-OK, Residence
Duration: Dramatic Action
Prerequisite charms: Ubiquitous Hearth Prana
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

The Lawgiver’s home is never empty. Their dearest friends and companions are always welcome at their hearths.

Activation of this Charm bestows the use of its prerequisite on another character as a Shaping effect. The character so favoured can use Ubiquitous Hearth Prana as if they were the Solar to travel to the Solar’s residence. If the target of this boon is a mortal, they use an activation cost of 3wp instead of the normal cost.

The Lunar’s immunity to Shaping creates difficulty in bestowing the favour on the Moon’s chosen, yet in the grand scheme of things this is but another hurdle to overcome.
