Immunity to Everything Technique

Costs: 6m, 1wp, Mins: Resistance 5, Essence 3, Type: Simple (Speed 4)
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: One Scene
Prerequisite charms: Any Resistance Excellency
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 208)

Not easily are the Lawgivers slain. This Charm stops points and sickness from doing further damage to the character for one scene. This Charm also prevents the character from suffering new Poison or Sickness effects during this scene. Effects that would poison or sicken him have no effect whatsoever. The character can walk hand-in-hand with plague victims, sprinkle poison on his food and eat ichneumon eggs as caviar. There are never ill effects later from these indiscretions.
