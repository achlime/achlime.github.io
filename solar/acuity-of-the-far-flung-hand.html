Acuity of the Far-Flung Hand

Costs: – (3m), Mins: Thrown 5, Essence 5, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Call the Blade (Thrown)
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 58), Scroll of Errata (p. 152-153)

This Charm costs three motes every time its benefits are invoked. It allows the character to hurl implements in order to take normal actions out to a range of (Essence x 10) yards; for example, the character could throw her lockpicks at a lock to make a normal Larceny roll, or hurl her daiklave at an opponent to make a standard Melee attack. Acuity of the Far-Flung Hand does not help the character retrieve tossed items. Penalties that would apply to a Thrown roll (such as as those due to wind or poor visibility) apply to the roll for the action taken.
