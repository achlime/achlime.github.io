Compendium Solar Ride

Flashing Thunderbolt Steed

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Ride 5, Essence 3, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Touch
Duration: One day
Prerequisite charms: Any Ride Excellency
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 236)

Essence imbues the Lawgiver’s mount with infinite energy. It can run all-out without suffering ill effects. It receives one automatic success on any roll to keep its footing or successfully jump an obstacle. With this Charm active, a mounted character can reliably cover (the horse’s Stamina x 10) miles per hour.
