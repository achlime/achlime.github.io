Compendium Solar Craft

Keen Understanding of the Core Imperfection

Costs: 10m, 1wp, Mins: Craft 5, Essence 4, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Touch
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Shattering Grasp
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 60), Scroll of Errata (p. 158)

The Solar touches a structure or object and gains a perfect understanding of what can damage or destroy it, and how well. Furthermore, for this Charm’s duration, her attacks ignore the target’s soak and deal to it (Essence × 5) additional dice of damage. If the object is completely indestructible, the Charm’s duration becomes Instant, and Lawgiver is instead granted divine inspiration, discovering an existing Flaw of Invulnerability (created by the Storyteller) that would permit the object’s destruction.
