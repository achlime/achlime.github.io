Thunderclap Rush Attack

Costs: 1m, Mins: Martial Arts 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Martial-ready (Archery, Melee, Thrown)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Fists of Iron Technique
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 190), Scroll of Errata (p. 21)

When the Solar Exalt strikes, she is swifter than lightning. The character pours Essence into quickening her actions. She then makes an unarmed Martial Arts attack. This Charm drops a supplemented attack’s Speed to 3.
