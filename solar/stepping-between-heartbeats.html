Compendium Solar Stealth

Stepping Between Heartbeats

Costs: —, Mins: Stealth 5, Essence 4, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Invisible Statue Spirit
Source: Glories of the Most High — the Unconquered Sun (p. 32)

This Charm permanently upgrades its prerequisite. While the character is still rendered visible by moving or participating in combat, these actions no longer deactivate Invisible Statue Spirit. The Lawgiver becomes invisible once more as soon as he remains stationary outside of combat for one tick.
