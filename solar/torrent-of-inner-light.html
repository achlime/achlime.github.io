Compendium Solar Thrown

Torrent of Inner Light

Costs: —, Mins: Thrown 5, Essence 4, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Falling Icicle Strike, Spirit Weapons, Triple-Distance Attack Technique
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 57-58), Scroll of Errata (p. 151)

The Solar is a living weapon, every mote of his Essence a microcosm of the perfect assassin. The character gains the following permanent benefits:

  • Triple Distance Attack Technique: The Charm extends the range of thrown weapons by a factor of ten rather than three. At Thrown 6+, Essence 6+ this Charm changes a weapon’s range value from yards to miles.
  • Spirit Weapons: This Charm’s Cost becomes zero motes. Additionally, all unexpected Thrown attacks ignore the target’s Hardness.
