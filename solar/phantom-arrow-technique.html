Compendium Solar Archery

Phantom Arrow Technique

Costs: — (1m per attack), Mins: Archery 3, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: N/A
Prerequisite charms: Essence Arrow Attack
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 189)

The bane of the mortal archer is her dependence on ammunition, but Lawgivers can use thhis Charm to shape sparkling arrows of Essence. This Charms permits a Lawgiver to make ranged attacks without using ammunition. Each such attack costs one mote of Essence. The Exaclt can attack as if using any form of ammunition without Resources cost, such as a broadhead, fowling, frog crotch or target arrow. This Charm permanently enhances the Exalt’s capabilities. Therefore, shaping motes into ammunition is an unrolled reflexive action and not a Charm activiation.
