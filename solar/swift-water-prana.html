Swift-Water Prana

Costs: 6m, Mins: Athletics 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

The lawgivers traverse the seas of creation with the same ease as their traversal of the lands of creation. Solar with this Charm active remove all external penalties due to being underwater, and no longer have their combat abilities capped by their Athletics. Furthermore, they can hold their breath underwater for (Essence × 10) minutes in addition to the normal duration.

This charm only works in natural water. Magical effects, such as Charms, spells, etc., that create environmental hazards that replicate natural water, but it isn’t actually natural. This is the same restrication as Element-Resisting Prana.
