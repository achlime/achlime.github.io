Compendium Solar Medicine

Ameliorated Physique Technique

Costs: 4m, Mins: Medicine 5, Essence 3, Type: Simple (Dramatic)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Shaping
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Medicine Excellency
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

Mortals under the care of the lawgiver often assume that many of life’s conditions are permanent. The Solar bathes his patients in golden light as he manipulates and changes their physique. Provided the Solar has a willing or unconscious patient and enough time to activate the Charm, he can change anything about the mortal’s appearance as long as it is naturally possible. This Charm does not allow the Solar to bestow mutations, so he cannot grant him the large mutation or change the target’s gender.

The Solar may use this Charm to alter the appearance of any willing or unconscious living creature. The creature may spend the Solar’s (Essence) in willpower to reject the changes the Solar made the charm’s completion should they foolishly disagree with the Solars designs. After this time, the changes are permanent and cannot be undone except by another application of this Charm. Depending on the severity of the changes, this Charm increases the difficulty to recognize the target with at most a +2.
