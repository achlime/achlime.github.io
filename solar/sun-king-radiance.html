Compendium Solar Integrity

Sun King Radiance

Costs: —, Mins: Integrity 5, Essence 4, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Elusive Dream Defense
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 201)

Others look to the Lawgivers for strength against temptation. This Charm allows characters loyal to the Solar to replace their Essence with the Solar’s Integrity score whan calculating mental defense. It subtracts two dice (as a -2 internal penalty) from the dice pools of characters loyal to the Solar when they are actively attempting to betray that loyalty. This Charm has no effect on Abyssal Exalted or other Solar Exalted.
