Compendium Solar Stealth

Shadow Presence Alibi

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Stealth 4, Essence 3, Type: Simple (Speed 5 in long ticks)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Illusion, Social
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Mental Invisibility Technique
Source: Custom (Roel Hendriks)

Sometimes it’s useful if people remember you being present, even if you weren’t. This Charm allows the lawgiver to duck out of a social event to pursue other goals, without those attending being any the wiser. The Exalt’s player rolls [(Wits or Manipulation) + Stealth], adding her Essence in automatic successes. This Charm exerts unnatural mental influence on all those who attend the social event. If the character’s sucesses exceed a target’s Dodge MDV, the target is under the illusion that the Solar never left the event and attended until its conclusion. The target must spend four Willpower to break the illusion, which drops to one Willpower if someone calls the target’s attention to the Solar’s absence, or if they notice the Solar leaving the event.
