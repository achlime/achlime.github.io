Compendium Solar Melee

Final Sunset Stance

Costs: 1m, Mins: Melee 5, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive (Step 2)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Martial-ready (Martial Arts), Native
Duration: One tick
Prerequisite charms: Dipping Swallow Defense
Source: Ink Monkeys Collection (p. 17)

The Bronze Tigers stand as a warning to the enemies of Creation: those who sow the wind reap the whirlwind. Using this Charm raises the character’s Melee-derived Parry DV by 1 for one tick. This bonus does not count as dice added by a Charm and ignores maximum DV limitations. Furthermore, any time a character using Final Sunset Stance successfully parries an attack with this DV, she stocks a free reflexive attack which she may withhold or unleash at a moment of her choosing. Free reflexive attacks may not be accrued with the aid of Charms bearing a Flaw of Invulnerability. Unused stocked attacks vanish from the Solar’s arsenal when the scene ends. A Solar may stock no more than (Dexterity + 3) attacks in this fashion through any combination of any Charms which stock free reflexive attacks, such as Shrike Saving Discretion (see p. 19).
