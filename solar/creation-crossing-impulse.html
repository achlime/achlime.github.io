Creation-Crossing Impulse

Costs: —, Mins: Athletics 5, Essence 4, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Racing Hare Method, Glorious Temple Body
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

This Charm upgrades Racing Hare Method, increasing the speed it allows to ([Stamina + Athletics + Essence] x 10) miles per hour. Additionally, the character can increase her travel speed further with Athletics Excellencies by increasing the static rating of her (Stamina + Athletics).
