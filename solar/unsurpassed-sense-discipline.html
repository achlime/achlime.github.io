Compendium Solar Awareness

Unsurpassed (Sense) Discipline

Costs: 2m, Mins: Awareness 5, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Keen (Sense) Technique
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 226)

Essence grants the character supreme sensory prowess. Unsurpassed (Sense) Discipline is actually three Charms. One that heightens sight, ont that heightens hearing and touch and one that heightens smell and taste.

This Charm doubles the character’s successes on Awareness rolls, before subtracting any external penalties. Should the character emply this Charm in combination with the appropriate Keen (Sense) Technique, it is reasonable to imagine that she could critique on the mating practices of insects, read with her fingertips or gauge an individual’s mood by scent.
