Compendium Solar Melee

Golden Destruction Cut

Costs: —, Mins: Melee 5, Essence 4, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Hungry Tiger Technique
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 56-57), Scroll of Errata (p. 150)

This Charm acts as a permanent upgrade to its prerequisite, allowing the Solar’s player to count extra successes on the attack roll three times rather than twice for the purpose of determining raw damage. A second purchase, at Melee 6+, allows extra successes to be counted four times rather than three, while a third purchase at Melee 8+ allows extra successes to be counted five times. A fourth and final purchase at Melee 9+ causes each extra success to be counted ten times for the purpose of determining raw damage.
