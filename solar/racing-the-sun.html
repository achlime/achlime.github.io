Compendium Solar Athletics

Racing the Sun

Costs: —, Mins: Athletics 4, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Lightning Speed
Source: Glories of the Most High — the Unconquered Sun (p. 31)

This Charm upgrades its prerequisite, granting it the Stackable keyword. Each additional use of Lightning Speed adds (Athletics) again to the Solar’s Move and Dash distances, though stacking more than one activation makes the Charm Obvious. The Solar may stack as many activations of Lightning Speed as her Essence pool permits.
