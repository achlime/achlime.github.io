Compendium Solar Resistance

Adamant Skin Technique

Costs: 8m, Mins: Resistance 5, Essence 4, Type: Reflexive (Step 7)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Dawn, Obvious, Mirror (Wounds Mean Nothing)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Spirit Strengthens the Skin, Iron Skin Concentration
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 207), Scroll of Errata (p. 25), Scroll of Errata (p. 20)

Driven by her purpose and her Virtues, the Exalt becomes invincible. The Solar invokes this Charm immediately before the damage of a physical attack or similar effect is rolled. This Charm is a perfect defense against the raw damage of the attack, reducing it to zero after all other effects. This Charm has one of the Four Flaws if Invulnerability (see p. 194).

As a point of clarification, this Charm (and its Abyssal Mirror Wounds Mean Nothing) reduces the raw and final damage of an incoming attack or similar phenomenon to zero after all other modifiers and effects. Damage added to an attack after soak is applied, such as by Fire and Stones Strike, cannot harm a character who activates Adamant Skin Technique.
