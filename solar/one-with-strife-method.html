One With Strife Method

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: War 5, Essence 4, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Counterattack
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Fury Inciting Presence
Source: Scroll of Errata (p. 152)

Some Solars learn to avoid conflict. Those of the Dawn Caste learn to revel in it. One With Strife Method enhances the Lawgiver’s capacity for violence, allowing him to use any of his combat abilities to make one free counterattack per action against each opponent that attacks him during that action.
