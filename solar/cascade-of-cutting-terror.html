Compendium Solar Thrown

Cascade of Cutting Terror

Costs: 5m, Mins: Thrown 3, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Triple-Distance Attack Technique
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 196)

In the First Age, not even the most elusive demons could escape the Solar’s judgement. This Charm supplements a Thrown-based attack, filling the air with dozens or hundres of duplicates of the Lawgiver’s thrown weapon. This Charm renders the target’s Dodge DV inapplicable against this attack. It also doubles the Solar’s successes on the attack roll before comparing it to defense, in Step 3 of attack resolution.
