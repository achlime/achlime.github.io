Nova Shell Ordnance

Costs: 2m; Mins: Firearms 2, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Screaming Wraith Bullet), Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Shards Of The Exalted Dream (p. 156)

A pulse of blinding white Essence explodes outward from the Solar’s bullet as it hits its mark. Nova Shell Ordnance adds (Essence) dice to the raw damage of a supplemented attack and grants it the Piercing tag. Against inanimate objects, this Charm halves their soak instead of simply subtracting from it.
