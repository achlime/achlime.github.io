Compendium Solar Larceny

Gilded Night Tools

Costs: 1m, Mins: Larceny 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple (Speed 3)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Stackable
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Any Larceny Excellency
Source: Custom (Roel Hendriks)

It would not do for one of the Iron Wolves to be left barred from bringing the tools they need. Why, it would be an utter embarassment. To prevent such inconvenience the Solar may store up to (Essence) of such items Elsewhere, to be called to her hand when she has need of them. This Charm allows the Solar to banish a set of tools for a specific Larceny use — such as lockpicking, disguising, smuggling or forgery — with every invocation, but can be used only for such tools. Recalling them from Elsewhere is a speed 6 miscellaneous action that ends the Charm.
