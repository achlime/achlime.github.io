Compendium Solar Larceny

Legitimate Merchant Approach

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Larceny 5, Essence 4, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Social, Illusion
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Larceny Excellency
Source: Custom (Roel Hendriks)

A Solar is not suspicious. A Solar does not smuggle. A Solar does not carry illegal cargo. A Solar has the needed papers, permits and stamps to pass through. Any claim to the contrary is wrong, for the evidence is right before the inspector’s eyes. A Solar that makes these claims and others may use this Charm to make her words more clear to the guard barring her way, and attempt to impose an Unnatural Mental Influence on those inspecting her and her cargo. To do so she rolls her ([Manipulation or Charisma] + Larceny), adding her Essence in automatic successes. If she exceeds her target’s Dodge MDV she has successfully put them under the illusion that there is no reason to hold her up any longer and to let her pass through. To break this illusion the target must spend four Willpower, or one Willpower if the target is confronted with strong evidence tying her directly to illegal activity she or her cargo are involved in.
