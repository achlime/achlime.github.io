Compendium Solar Archery

Solar Flare Methodology

Costs: 3m, 1wp or 5m, 1wp, Mins: Archery 4, Essence 4, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Holy, Obvious
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Essence Arrow Attack
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 189), Scroll of Errata (p. 21)

The Solar shapes his Essence into a firewand. This Charm is identical to Glorious Solar Saber, except that it creates artifact firewands and similar weapons. Examples include the plasma tongue repeater (see Wonders of the Lost Age, p. 75) or the prayer piece (see Glories of the Most High: The Unconquered Sun, p. 38).
