Compendium Solar Thrown

Joint-Wounding Attack

Costs: 2m, Mins: Thrown 3, Essence 1, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Crippling, Martial-ready (Archery, Martial Arts, Melee)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 195), Scroll of Errata (p. 24)

Even in a pitched battle, the Lawgivers strike with controlled precision. The Charm enhances a Thrown-based attack so that it stuns or cripples the Solar’s opponent. For each health level of damage this attack inflicts, this Charm subtracts one die (as a -1 internal penalty) from the target’s Physical Attribute dice pools and one point from the equivalent static ratings. This is a Crippling effect that lasts for the remainder of the scene. The players and Storyteller decide how the effect is inflicted—it could be an injured hand, slashed tendons or a stunning blow to the head. Storytellers will have to work with the players to determine how the attack cripples more exotic opponents such as automata and the walking dead.
