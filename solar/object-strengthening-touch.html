Compendium Solar Craft

Object-Strengthening Touch

Costs: 5m, Mins: Craft 2, Essence 1, Type: Simple (Speed 5)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Touch
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Any Craft Excellency
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 211)

The things of the world strive to serve the Solars well. This Charm makes an object more difficult to break. It increases the number of successes necessary to damage the object by the character’s Essence. It increases the (Strength + Athletics) rating necessary to break the object with a feat of strength by the character’s Essence. No combination of Charms that include Object-Strengthening Touch can increase these totals by more than the character’s Essence.
