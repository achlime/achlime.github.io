Wind-Racing Essence Infusion

Costs: —, Mins: Ride 5, Essence 4, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Flashing Thunderbolt Steed
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 236)

The steeds of the Lawgivers possess extraordinary speed. This Charm enhances the character’s Flashing Thunderbolt Steeds technique, allowing the mount under its influence to reliably travel ([the creature’s Stamina + the character’s Essence] x 10) miles per hour.
