Compendium Solar Melee

Magic Channeling Strike

Costs: —, Mins: Melee 3, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Any Melee Excellency
Source: Custom (Roel Hendriks), Custom (Brend Wanders)

The swords of Lawgivers become an extension of their body. This Charm allows the Solar to perform the touch required by the Touch keyword with a held melee weapon. Touching a non-consenting target with a held weapon requires a (Dexterity + Melee) attack. This Charm is explicitly allowed to affect actions from other Abilities. Simply having this Charm is not Obvious, but use of this Charm is Obvious if the Touch-keyworded Charm is Obvious.
