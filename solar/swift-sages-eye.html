Swift Sage’s Eye

Costs: 2m, Mins: Linguistics 2, Essence 1, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Whirling Brush Method
Source: Custom (Roel Hendriks)

Drawing on his keen understanding of words, the Exalt lets his essence aid him in reading a text. When performing an action to read a book, scroll or other written record he can read as fast as his eyes can track across the words, up to at most (Linguistics + Essence) pages per second.
