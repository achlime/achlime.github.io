Compendium Solar Medicine

Salient Purity Prana

Costs: 12m, 1wp per point of new Breeding rating, Mins: Medicine 5, Essence 4, Type: Simple (Dramatic Action)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, Touch, Training
Duration: One Week
Prerequisite charms: Ameliorated Physique Technique
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

Under the banner of the Solar Exalted, the Terrestrials were allowed to sully their blood by cavorting their Celestial superiors and mortal inferiors. Few of the Lawgivers realized the impact of their baser desires, and even less cared.

This Charm can only target Dragon-Blooded. The activation of this Charm costs an additional amount of temporary Willpower equal to the target’s (Breeding + 1). This Charm requires five or more hours of effort in any given day of the Charm’s duration to bear fruit. The Solar’s player rolls (Intelligence + Medicine) against a difficulty of the target’s (Breeding + 1). Success raises the target’s Breeding by one dot as a Training effect (maximum Breeding 5).

The Solar can use this Charm only once per season. Furthermore, after a succesful application of the Charm a Dragon-blooded can only benefit from Salient Purity Prana again after they have raised their Essence, and at least (new Breeding × 2) seasons have passed since their Breeding was raised.
