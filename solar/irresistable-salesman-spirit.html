Compendium Solar Presence

Irresistable Salesman Spirit

Costs: 3m, Mins: Presence 4, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-Basic, Social
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Presence Excellency
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 204)

The glamour of the Lawgivers inspires others to dream of glorious things. This makes the Solars the ultimate salesmen and recruiters. Irresistable Salesman Spirit enhances a Presence-based roll to exert mental influence and make a favorable bargain or encourage a specific action. This Charm doubles the Solar’s successes on the roll before comparing them to the target’s MDV.
