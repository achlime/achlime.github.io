Compendium Solar Melee

Call the Blade

Costs: (1m), Mins: Melee 2, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Merged (Call the Blade (Thrown)), Obvious, Mirror (Blade-Summoning Gesture)
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 191), Scroll of Errata (p. 22)

The Solar’s weapons know their master. The Solar holds out her hand and calls to the Essence of one of her weapons. If the desired weapon is within (Essence * 10) yards, and a flight path exists between the weapon and her hand, this Charm draes the weapon into her grasp. She must own the weapon she calls. This Charm can be used to draw and ready a sheathed weapon reflexively.

The Solar may reflexively spend one mote to activate this Charm as described. He may only call his blade from up to (Essence * 2) yards away while in battle, however.
