Compendium Solar Integrity

Should the Sun Not Rise

Costs: —, Mins: Integrity 6, Essence 6, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Holy, Native
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Scroll of Errata (p. 154)

In eternal night, the Solar Exalted are evidence of a perfect light that once was and shall be again. Should the sun go down forever, the Lawgivers stand as proof of an imperishable hope, surpassing the light from whence they sprang. By learn ing this Charm, the Solar substantiates for himself the foundational principles of Holy by which the universe abides and the Unconquered Sun decides. In the event of the death of the Unconquered Sun, the Solar’s Holy Charms retain their full capacity against all creatures of darkness. This Charm also permanently lowers the cost to activate all Charms with the Holy Keyword by one mote, to a minimum of zero motes.
