Fire and Stones Strike

Costs: 1m per die, Mins: Melee 3, Essence 1, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Hungry Tiger Technique
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 190)

The Solar demonstrates his strength with a powerful Essence-fuelled blow. The Exalt spends up to (his Strength score) motes and makes a Melee-based attack. If the attack hits, this Charm adds one die to the post-soak damage for each mote spent.
