Compendium Solar Linguistics

Conference in Tranquility

Costs: 5m+, Mins: Linguistics 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Stackable, Mirror (Screaming in Silence)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

This Solar mirror of Screaming in Silence requires that the targets are willing, and does not allow a roll to establish contact of the target resists. Instead, the Solar can opt to invite additional individuals, at a surcharge of 2m per additional individual beyond the first target.

Up to the characters (Linguistics) additional characters may be invited. The Charm functions even if not all invitees accept, those that resists are simply not part of the mental convesation. Multiple activations do not create a single network, but instead require the Solar to carry on multiple mental conversations – which comes with the same problems as carrying on multiple normal conversations.
