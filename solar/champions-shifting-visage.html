Compendium Solar Integrity

Champion’s Shifting Visage

Costs: 1+m, Mins: Integrity 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple (Speed 3)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Shaping
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Integrity-Protecting Prana
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

Upon activation of this Charm, the Solar commits 1 mote per (Essence) mutations, or part thereof, she currently carries. All her mutations are then supressed as a Shaping effect, restoring her to her original form. She receives no benefits, nor disadvantages, from her mutations and her appearance is as if she has no mutations at all.

The Solar can then recall her true form with a miscellaneous action. The Charm ends when she does so. If she cancels the Essence committed to this Charm while her mutations are supressed they return immediately, but she suffers one level of unsoakable Bashing damage due to the sudden change.
