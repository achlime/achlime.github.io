Compendium Solar Linguistics

Guiding Words of Solar Wisdom

Costs: 15m, 1wp, Mins: Linguistics 5, Essence 4, Type: Simple (Dramatic Action)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Training
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Letter-Within-a-Letter Technique
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 65), Scroll of Errata (p. 170)

The Lawgiver passes on her holy insight. Perhaps by writing a tome or inventing a mantra, she gives a lesson focused on a number of techins equal to her Linguistics. (These teachines may include specific Abilities, Charms, Essence, et cetera.) Those why stude her teachings may increase those traits as though she were tutoring them.

This Charm may explicitly be placed in a Combo with any Training-Keyword Charm, even in violation of standard Combo rules, permitting those effects to be encoded into the resultant magical training manual.
