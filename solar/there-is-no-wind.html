Compendium Solar Archery

There is No Wind

Costs: 3m or 5m, Mins: Archery 4, Essence 1, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Martial (Thrown), Mirror (Pulse of the Prey)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 187), Scroll of Errata (p. 20)

The Solar’s heart knows the arrow’s path. She spends three motes and fires a single flawless shot, regardless of distance, visibility, weather and other prevailing conditions. This Charm nullifies all penalties, except wound and multiple action penalties, applying to a single Archery-based attack. This Charm does not negate DVs.

If the Solar has Essence 3 or higher, she can spend two additional motes and this Charm will increase the Range of her weapon to her maximum visibility range. The Essence 3+ upgrade does not apply to Thrown.
