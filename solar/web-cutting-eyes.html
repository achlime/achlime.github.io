Web-Cutting Eyes

Costs: 5m, Mins: Socialize 5, Essence 5, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Merciless Dissecting Stare)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Mastery of Small Manners
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 66), Scroll of Errata (p. 172)

The Solar’s cunning gaze cuts across all subtleties of motive and concealment, laying bare the intentions of those who stand before him. While this Charm is active, all attempts to read the motivation of other characters (see Exalted, p. 131) are made at difficulty 1.

New Abyssal Charm: Merciless Dissecting Stare (Prerequisites: Exquisite Etiquette Style). This Charm is identical to its Solar counterpart.
