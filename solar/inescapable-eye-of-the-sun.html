Inescapable Eye of the Sun

Costs: 3m, Mins: Awareness 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Touch
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Any Awareness Excellency
Source: Glories of the Most High — the Unconquered Sun (p. 31)

The vigilance of the Lawgivers is as inescapable as day and night. By touching a solid obstruction such as a door or wall no more than (Essence) yards thick, the Solar is able to perceive any events occurring on the other side as though he were present. Breaking contact with the surface through which one is spying ends the Charm.
