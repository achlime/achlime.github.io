First (Ability) Excellency

Costs: 1m per die, Mins: (Ability) 1, Essence 1, Type: Reflexive (Step 1 for attacker, Step 2 for defender)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (First (Ability) Excellency)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 183-184)

Lawgivers with this Charm are known for their moments of brilliance. The Exalt’s player can invoke this charm whan making a roll based on the relevant Ability. This Charm then adds one die to that roll for each mote spent. This Charm is the paradigmatic dice adder. The rule that Solars are prohibited from increasing a dice pool by more than their (Attribute + Ability) almost always refers to the purchase of dice through the use of this Charm.

Characters can also use this Charm to enhance unrolled uses of the relevant Ability, usually to increase a Defense Value, for example, by increasing (Dexterity + Dodge) to protect against a physical attack. When enhancing static values, her player rolls one die for each mote spent on the First Excellency. Each success increases her effective (Attribute + Ability) rating for the task by two. Therefore, each success on the First Excellency roll increases an applicable static value by one.

Characters cannot use this or other Excellencies or dice-adding Charms to “create” actions. For example, if a character attacks on tick 3 and will attack again on tick 8, she cannot buy dice for an attack on tick 4 that only exists because she used the Excellency to conjure dice for it. A character who uses this or other reflexive Charms to add to an attack can also use it to enhance her defense until the next when she acts, but she must spend Essence for each separate roll she wishes to modify.

This Charm can be part of a Combo or used together with the Second Excellency, but it is incompatibly with the Third Excellency. It can never be used on the same roll as the Third Excellency.
