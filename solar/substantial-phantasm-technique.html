Substantial Phantasm Technique

Costs: — (4m), Mins: Performance 4, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: N/A
Prerequisite charms: Phantom-Conjuring Performance
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

This Charm enhances its prerequisite to allow the creation of caresses, touch, friction, and other forms of physical presence. These effects can not be used to attack with, but can be used as part of a social attack requiring touch at a surcharge of 4m.
