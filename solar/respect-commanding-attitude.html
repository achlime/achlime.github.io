Compendium Solar Performance

Respect Commanding Attitude

Costs: 5m, Mins: Performance 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple (Speed 4 in long ticks)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Compulsion, Social
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 202)

Others listen hwne the Lawgivers speak. In ancient days, they commanded the attiont of Primordials, beasts and gods. This Charm involves a performance of oration, and the Solar’s player rolls (an appropriate Social Attribute + Performance) when invoking Respect Command Attitude. This Charm exerts unnatural mental influence on everyone who can see or hear the character. If they extra successes exceed a given target’s MDV, that target suffers a compulsion to stay in the Exalt’s company and respectfully observe the performance. Resisting this compulsion for five minutes costs on Willpower, up to a maximum of three Willpower per scene, and such ressitance allows the target to leave, heckle or even forcibly silence the character.
