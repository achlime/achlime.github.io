Compendium Solar Resistance

Spirit Strengthens the Skin

Costs: 1m per pre-soak damage die removed, Mins: Resistance 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 7)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Durability of Oak Meditation
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 207), Scroll of Errata (p. 25)

The unconquerable flam of the Solar’s spirit armors her against attack. She invokes this Charm after an attack hits her but before damage is rolled. This Charm reduces the post-soak damage of the attack by one die for each mote spent. It may not reduce an attack’s damage below one die.
