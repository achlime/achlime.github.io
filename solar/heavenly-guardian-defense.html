Compendium Solar Melee

Heavenly Guardian Defense

Costs: 8m, Mins: Melee 4, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 2)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Bulwark Stance
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 193), Scroll of Errata (p. 24)

The character invokes this Charm in response to an attack. The attack must not be unexpected, and the character must have a weapon in hand. This Charm is a parry that perfectly defends agains any attack, even if it is unblockable.

Mundane weapons can break when used with this Charm. If the character uses this Charm and a mundane weapon to block an attack that inflicts at least 25L raw damage before soak, the weapon breaks (though it still successfully parries).

This Charm has one of the Four Flaws of Invulnerability (see boxed text) common to all Solar perfect defences.
