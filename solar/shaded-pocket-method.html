Compendium Solar Larceny

Shaded Pocket Method

Costs: 1m, Mins: Larceny 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Stackable, Touch
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Flawless Pickpocketing Technique
Source: Custom (Roel Hendriks)

Even in the brightest sunshine, shadows can be cast dark and deep. A Solar who seeks to hide an object she may be searched for may use this Charm to hide it perfectly, automatically succeeding to hide the object. If this effect is opposed by another Charm or magical effect, add the character’s Essence in automatic successes to the (Dexterity + Larceny) roll to oppose the rival magic.

This Charm allows the Solar to hide items that she does not own; if she tries to do so she must first establish control over the object by either stealing it or by successfully disarming the wielder. This Charm can be stacked up to (Essence) times but cannot hide items that would be too large for her to hide without the use of Charms. Retrieving an item hidden by this Charm from its hiding place is a speed 4 miscellaneous action, ending the Charm in the process
