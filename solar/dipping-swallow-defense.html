Dipping Swallow Defense

Costs: 2m, Mins: Melee 2, Essence 1, Type: Reflexive (Step 2)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Martial (Martial Arts), Mirror (Elegant Flowing Deflection)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 192), Scroll of Errata (p. 23)

The manoeuvre named Dipping Swallow Defence restores defensive advantage to a beleaguered or overextended fighter. This Charm is used in response to an attack. It allows the Exalt to ignore all penalties that apply to her Melee-derived Parry DV when resolving that attack. Her Parry DV is still 0 if it’s inapplicable, but it takes no further penalties.
