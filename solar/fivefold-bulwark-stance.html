Fivefold Bulwark Stance

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Melee 5, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Martial (Martial Arts), Mirror (Eye of the Tempest)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Bulwark Stance
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 194), Scroll of Errata (p. 24)

The world weeps when the Chosen fall, and so, the Unconquered Sun has given them every master of defence. For the remainder of the scene, this Charm removes the onslaught penalty other characters’ attacks impose on the Exalt’s DV. It also reduces the defence penalty imposed by each action the Exalt takes by one, to a minimum of zero.

These penalties are only applied to the Exalt’s Melee-derived Parry DV.
