Compendium Solar War

Rout-Stemming Gesture

Costs: 4m, 1wp, Mins: War 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, War
Duration: Until next action
Prerequisite charms: Any War Excellency
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 197)

The glory of the Solar Exalted steadies those who follow them. The Exalt can target up to her Essence in military units or (Essence × 100) scattered individuals with this Charm. The Exalt chooses the target insofar as she cares to specify; otherwise, this Charm affects the closest non-enemy targets. Individual targets must be able to see or hear the Exalt. Targeted units must be able to see or hear some sort of Solar-sent signal. This Charm cases the targets to succeed automatically on all Valor rolls. If used in the instant one ore more targets fail a Valor roll, this Charm allows the targets that failed to reroll, but the reroll does not automatically succeed. This Charm’s effects last until the Exalt’s next combat or mass combat action.
