Compendium Solar Presence

Authority-Radiating Stance

Costs: 3m, Mins: Presence 4, Essence 3, Type: Simple (Speed 4 in long ticks)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Social
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Majestic Radiant Presence
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 204)

The Solar assumes the mantle of authority. This Charm is a form of unnatural mental influence. For the rest of the scene, each character who interacts personally with the Exlat and has a lower MDV than the Exalt’s Presence suffers an unnatural Illusion effect. He believes that the Exalt is someone with authority over him. Recognizing this belief as an illusion costs two Willpower. This Charm, as specific exception to the usualy rules for unnatural effects, does not cost Limit to resist.
