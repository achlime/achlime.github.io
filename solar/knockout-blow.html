Compendium Solar Martial Arts

Knockout Blow

Costs: 4m, Mins: Martial Arts 4, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Crippling, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Solar Hero Form
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 190), Scroll of Errata (p. 21)

Solar Exalted with this Charm can topple even behemoths with a single punch. This Charm supplements an unarmed Martial Arts attack. This Charm takes effect if the attack hits and inflicts even one health level of damage. If [damage successes × Exalt’s Essence] is greater than the target’s remaining health levels, this Charm knocks the target unconscious, and the target remains so for a scene.
