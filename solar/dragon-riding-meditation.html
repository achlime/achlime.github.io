Dragon-Riding Meditation

Costs: —, Mins: Ride 5, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Worthy Mount Technique
Source: Glories of the Most High — the Unconquered Sun (p. 33)

While mere animals suffice to carry the youngest of Lawgivers into battle, more experienced heroes bend lesser legends to their service. This Charm permanently enhances all of a Solar’s Ride Charms, allowing them to be invoked to enhance a sentient, consenting mount.
