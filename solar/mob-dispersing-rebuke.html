Mob-Dispersing Rebuke

Costs: 7m, 1wp, Mins: War 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple (Speed 3 in long ticks)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Social, War
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 197)

The Lawgiver chastises the unworthy rabble. This Charm forces the player of an enemy unit most of whose members are within (Essence × 100) yards to make an immediate check for rout. The difficulty of this roll is 1 if the unit has Magnitude greater than the Exalt’s Essence, and 2 otherwise. This Charm can be used socially rather than in war, in which case the Exalt can treat any mob, gathering or social group he can reasonably chastise as the target military unit.
