Compendium Solar Thrown

Spirit Weapons

Costs: 4m, Mins: Thrown 4, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive (Step 1)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Call the Blade
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 197), Scroll of Errata (p. 24)

The Exalt shapes weapons from shadows, Essence and light. This Charm permits a Lawgiver to create temporary throwing weapons. The Solar can reflexively create new weapons at any time as long as he has hands free to hold them. Each has the weapon qualities of a Throw weapon costing at most Resouces 2. Weapons created by this Charm area always exceptional. These weapons last for one scene, but they thrive on the Essence of the Exalt who created them. If anyone else uses them to make an attack, these weapons fade away during Step 2 of the attack’s resolution.
