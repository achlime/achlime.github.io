Compendium Solar Occult

Magic-Breaking Blow

Costs: 15m, 1wp per Spell Circle, Mins: Occult 5, Essence 4, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, Touch
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: All-Encompassing Sorcerer’s Sight
Source: Custom (Roel Hendriks)

Developed early in the Primordial War this Charm offers the Solar a defense against the world-twisting powers of the Primordials, albeit a limited one. Magic-Breaking Blow permits the Solar to disrupt sorcery, necromancy, and Sorcerous-keyword Charms. The Charm or spell must be Obvious to the Lawgiver’s senses to be a valid target for Magic-Breaking Blow. The Lawgiver targets a spell or appropriate Charm and spends 15 motes and an amount of Willpower equal to the target’s Spell Circle. His player rolls (Wits + Occult) against a difficulty of (Circle x 3). If successful, the effect is dispelled as if it had been struck by a Counter Magic Spell of the same level. For magical effects created by Sorcerous keyworded Charms, refer to the keyword and Charm text for their Circle equivalent and their effects upon dispelling.

Note that while the various Summoning Spells are valid targets—as are the bindings imposed by those spells upon the creatures summoned by them—the creatures themselves are not.
