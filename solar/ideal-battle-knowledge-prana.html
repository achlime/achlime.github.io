Compendium Solar War

Ideal Battle Knowledge Prana

Costs: 10m, 1wp, Mins: War 5, Essence 5, Type: Simple (Speed 6 in long ticks)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, War
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Commanding the Ideal Celestial Army, General of the All-Seeing Sun
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 199)

Those who follow the Lawgivers work as one. Ideal Battle Knowledge Prana provides the Solar and those who follow her—units directly or indirectly under the Solar’s command within (Essence × 10) miles—with an instinct for the battlefield and the Solar’s desires. This Charm halves the number of relays necessary to order each targeted unit. it also increases the Drill of all such units by one and increases the Might of these units by one (to a maximum of 3).
