Compendium Solar Mirage Tree

Mirage Tree

Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

This charm tree features charms building from Phantom-Conjuring Performance to allow the Solar to make persistent mirages, and to interact with the world via such mirages.

Mirage

:

Mirages are illusory images created from the Solar’s own Essence. As such they count as an arcane link to the Solar. Any knowledge the mirage gains and any experiences it has are separate from the Solar. Unless otherwise stated, the memories of the mirage become know the to Solar if the mirage merges with him, ending its existence. The mirage has the same traits as the Solar that created it, but it cannot use Charms or other Essence-fueled effects. Mirages have a single -0 Health Level, and are destroyed upon receiving any damage. A mirage is a kind of automaton. They are completely immune to all forms of disease and poison, natural or supernatural. They do not require sleep, and cannot become fatigued as a result of exertion. They do not need air, food, water or any other form of sustenance.

City Spanning Performance Method

Costs: 6m, 1wp, Mins: Performance 5, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, Social
Duration: One Action
Prerequisite charms: Phantom-Conjuring Performance
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

The word of the Lawgivers cannot be escaped. This Charm may be activated to have translucent golden likenesses of the Solar spring into being to deliver their words to everyone in the area, inescapable.

For the druation of the Charm, any Performance-based social attacks (or simple declarations) made by the Solar are transmitted to everyone within (Essence) miles in every direction. Because the Solar cannot see or hear through the ethereal manifestations, social counterattacks that require the target to be present may not be invoked in response to this Charm.

Substantial Phantasm Technique

Costs: — (4m), Mins: Performance 4, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: N/A
Prerequisite charms: Phantom-Conjuring Performance
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

This Charm enhances its prerequisite to allow the creation of caresses, touch, friction, and other forms of physical presence. These effects can not be used to attack with, but can be used as part of a social attack requiring touch at a surcharge of 4m.

Incandescent Apparition Technique

Costs: 3m, Mins: Performance 4, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Phantom-Conjuring Performance
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

With a moment’s concentration, the Solar’s Essence renders an illusion created by Phantom-Conjuring Performance permanent and imperishable. This Charm may enchant any kind of illusion that incorporates a visual element, from a simple visual apparition to many overlaying sensory effects. While the Essence remains committed to this Charm, the apparition cannot be erased or defaced. Merely damaging the apparition’s surroundings does nothing to remove it, but outright destruction of the object or building that forms the context for the apparition will remove it. The apparition shines through anything placed over it as though it were a Solar caste mark shining at the four- to seven-mote level of display, and is faintly luminous in the dark.

Brilliant Ephemeral Echoes

Costs: 5m, Mins: Performance 5, Essence 4, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, Social
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Incandescent Apparition Technique
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

Creation reverbs with the perfected performances of the Solars. With a closing flourish, the Solar’s Essence renders his performance imperishable. As long as Phantom-Conjuring Performance was used in the performance this Charm may enchant anything from a short poetry reading, to a six-hours dance accompanied by an orchestra.

While Essence remains committed to the Charm, shimmering echoes of the performance will remain at the location. Anyone that happens upon the location is subjected to the performance attack as it was performed originally. The echoes can only affect a character once, encountering the echo a second time has no effect. Furthermore, if the original performance ran for a longer duration, nothing prevents characters from leaving before the echo concludes unless they are compelled to stay.

At Essence 5+ this Charm allows a surcharge of 3m and 1wp to allow the performance echo to affect the same characters more than once, but it will be limited to affecting the original targets only.

Thousand Handshakes Stance

Costs: 1+m, 1wp, Mins: Performance 5, Essence 4, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Social, Mirage
Duration: One Scene
Prerequisite charms: Substantial Phantasm Technique, City Spanning Performance Method
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

The challenges of the Chosen of the Sun are many, and the Chosen meet them all with shining confidence. Yet sometimes, even the Chosen need some assistance. If the Solar is called upon to interview all people at the market, to negotiate with all bureaucrats in the palace, or is expected to simultaneously meet with the many ladies of the court, this Charm supplements the social activity undertaken by the Solar.

At a cost of 1m per mirage, the Charm can create up to (Essence x 5) mirages from the Solar’s own Esence. The mirages persist for the duration of the Charm, and can move freely within (Essence x 100) yards of the Solar. The Charm ends when the activity ends or is interrupted. With respect to the action the images are created for, it is as if the Solar himself is present in multiple locations. The mirages are not capable of taking up other activities, but the Solar has complete control over the mirages within the bounds of the activity.

The Solar is aware and can experience all that the mirages he can observe directly experience. Should the Solar want to retain the memories of mirages that are not observed by him, he can have them make their way back to him to be re-absorbed at the end of the activity.

At the Solar’s choice the mirages are either exact likenesses of the Solar, requiring a difficulty 3 (Perception + Awareness) toll to discern that they are illusions, or they can be shimmering golden phantasms, providing a clear display of the powers of the Chosen of the Sun.

This Charm is explicitly allowed to supplement actions with other abilities.

Eidolon Condensing Prana

Costs: 10m, 2wp, Mins: Performance 5, Essence 5, Type: Simple (Speed 5 in long ticks)
Keywords: Mirage
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Substantial Phantasm Technique, Incandescent Apparition Technique
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

To the Lawgivers, no number of concurrent appointments is too many. Even being in two places across creation is not impossible for the Chosen of the Sun.

Activating this Charm, the Solar spends five minutes in deep meditation to shape an Essence-based mirage of himself. These mirages are semi-independent extension of the Solar’s will. The mirages share his personality, goals, traits and essentially operates as him. They do not consider themselves to be independent entities. The mirages are free to travel away from the Solar, and will persist as long as the motes remain committed.

The mirage does not need food, air or sleep, but can act is if it does. The mirage gets 2 automatic successes on rolls to act as if it is a normal living being. Anyone interacting with the mirage must succeed at a difficulty 4 (Perception + Awareness) roll to realize that this is not the actual Solar. Even if they succeed they find it difficult to point out exactly what is off.

The Solar is not aware the memories and experiences of the mirage. In order to absorb the memories of anything learned by a mirage, the Solar must touch the mirage and spend a miscellaneous action (Speed 5, DV -0) re-absorbing it. If a mirage is destroyed, or if the committed motes are cancelled, the memories are irretrievably lost.
