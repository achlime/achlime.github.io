Compendium Solar Resistance

Ox-Body Technique

Costs: —, Mins: Resistance 1, Essence 1, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Stackable
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 208)

The Exalted are like mountains: not easily worn down by such mortal things as men. This Charm gives the Lawgiver additional health levels.

A player may purchase this Charm up to once per dot of the Resistance Ability her character possesses. Each purchase provides one of the following, at the player’s option, determined at the time of purchase:

  • One -0 health level
  • Two -1 health levels
  • One -1 health level and two -2 health levels
