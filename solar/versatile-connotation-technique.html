Compendium Solar Linguistics

Versatile Connotation Technique

Costs: 6m, Mins: Linguistics 4, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Insidious Undertone Trick)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Conference in Tranquility, Letter-Within-a-Letter Technique
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders)

A Lawgiver with this Charm can embed a hidden message in their conversation or utterance, this need not be a short statement—just like a Letter-Within-a-Letter message can span a whole book. Only when this Charm is used for a direct Manipulation-based attack does it gain the Social keyword and change to type Simple (Speed 6 in long ticks).
