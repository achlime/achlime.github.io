Arrow Pennant Aegis

Costs: —, Mins: Melee 3, Essence 1, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Ink Monkeys Collection (p. 17)

The Lawgiver’s skill with a shield may even guard her against a bolt from the blue. When the character uses a shield to respond to ranged attacks from outside her engagement range she gains the following benefits:

  • Her Melee-derived Parry DV is increased by one; this bonus does not count as dice added by a Charm and ignores maximum DV limitations.
  • Melee Charms used to block such attacks cost one mote less, to a minimum of 0 motes.
