Compendium Solar Archery

Forceful Arrow

Costs: 1m, Mins: Archery 4, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Knockback, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: There is No Wind
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 187), Scroll of Errata (p. 20)

Essence imbues the Lawgiver’s arrow with great force. This Charm supplements and Archery-based attack. If an attack enhanced by this Charm strikes its target and rolls even one die of damage, the target is automatically knocked back (archer’s Essence × 3) yards without opportunity to resist. Objects are knocked back automatically if (the raw damage × 50) exceeds the object’s weight in pounds.

A character who has learned the Charm Lightning Strikes Twice may apply the effects of that Charm to a knockback cuased by Forceful Arrow. See Ink Monkeys, Vol. 33 for the Charm Lightning Strikes Twice.
