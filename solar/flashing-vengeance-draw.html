Compendium Solar Archery

Flashing Vengeance Draw

Costs: — (3m), Mins: Archery 3, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Martial-ready (Martial Arts, Melee, Thrown), Mirror (Doom Drawn and Imminent)
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Trance of Unhesitating Speed
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 188), Scroll of Errata (p. 21)

To assault the Chosen of the Unconquered Sun is folly. Their retribution is swift and terrible. The Exalt must have an Archer weapon available for use to invoke this Charm, but it need not be in her hands. This Charm enhances the Join Battle action, giving the Solar a number of bonus successes on the Join Battle roll equal to her Essence. This Charm immediately and reflexively draws and readies an available Archery weapon.

The Solar invokes the described benefits by paying three motes. In addition, if the character rolls the highest Join Battle when using this Charm at the start of the fight, the first attack he makes following Join Battle is unexpected. The Solar must gain the highest result; ties spoil this effect.
