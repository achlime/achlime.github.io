Piston-Driven Megaton Hammer

Costs: 3m [1m], Mins: Strength 2, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Strength Augmentation
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 122)

This Charm takes the form of a soulsteel-capped piston ringed with Essence injection vents in one of the Alchemical’s limbs. Upon making a close combat attack with the selected limb, the piston may be triggered with a terrible whistling shriek of steam and an explosive bang. Successes on the supplemented attack roll are counted twice for the purpose of determining raw damage, or three times if attacking unarmed.

Chaoentropic Rending System

(6xp):

This voidtech submodule upgrades Piston-Driven Megaton Hammer (see pp. 122-123), whenever an attack enhanced by that Charm inflicts three or more levels of non-bashing damage, the Apostate may inflict an amputation wound on the target (see Exalted, p. 152 for examples). Doing so with a close combat attack allows the Apostate to rip the organ out and stuff it into his distended mouth, restoring four motes. Organs ripped from corpses offer no nourishment, so a killing attack offers only one feeding opportunity. If the enhanced attack mortally wounds or kills an animate being, its body and Essence shreds into a smoking mass of gore that leaves only indestructible possessions intact. Such death is permanent for spirits.

Optimized Demolition Vibration

(Essence 3, 2xp):

This submodule allows attacks made with fighting gauntlets, iron boots, their artifact equivalents and similar “brawling aids” such as tiger claws to be treated as unarmed attacks for the purposes of this Charm.
