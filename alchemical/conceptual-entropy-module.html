Compendium Alchemical Social

Conceptual Entropy Module

Costs: 6m, 1wp [1m], Mins: Manipulation 4, Essence 3, Type: Simple (6 long ticks)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Compulsion
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Manipulation Augmentation
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 152)

The Alchemical has a spiked gland of soulsteel and starmetal wires buried in her brain, attached to a tiny transmitter diode located just below her ear. It emits a corrosive subliminal pulse when triggered, tipping popular opinion away from an agenda the Exalt opposes. This Charm functions identically to Taboo Inflicting Diatribe (see Exalted, p. 238) with the following exceptions:

It may only be used to encourage the rejection of a current Intimacy within the group’s current Policy. The targeted Intimacy is removed and cannot be favorably reimplemented as part of the group’s Policy until its leader spends a total of six Loyalty to allow the rebuilding of that Intimacy to begin. Only one Loyalty can be spent in this manner per day.
