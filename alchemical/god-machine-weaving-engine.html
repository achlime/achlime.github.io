God-Machine Weaving Engine

Costs: — [1m], Mins: Intelligence 5, Essence 5, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Exemplar (1), Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Man-Machine Weaving Engine
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 170)

A second, larger ring of adamant surrounds the Alchemical’s lesser Weaving Engine. While weaving a God-Machine protocol, both rings turn in opposite directions, with a brilliant storm of sparks jumping between them and arcing into the Exalt’s soulgem. This Charm may never be removed once installed, but it does not function for characters infected with Gremlin Syndrome.

The character may take Weave God-Machine Protocol actions, which are identical to Shape Celestial Circle Sorcery actions save that they invoke a God-Machine protocol rather than a spell. This Charm cannot be placed in an Array.
