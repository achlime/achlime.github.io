Compendium Alchemical

Any (Attribute) Augmentation

Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 119-121)

This prerequisite is satisfied if the Alchemical knows any of the alternative Charms listed. The alternative charm must be known for the appropriate Attribute.

Possible alternatives:
  • First (Attribute) Augmentation
  • Second (Attribute) Augmentation
  • Third (Attribute) Augmentation
  • Fourth (Attribute) Augmentation
  • Fifth (Attribute) Augmentation
  • Sixth (Attribute) Augmentation
