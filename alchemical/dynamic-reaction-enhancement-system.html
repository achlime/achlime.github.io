Compendium Alchemical Speed and Mobility

Dynamic Reaction Enhancement System

Costs: 2m [1m], Mins: Dexterity 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Variable
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any (Attribute) Augmentation
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 141-142), Scroll of Errata (p. 96)

This Charm takes the form of a complex mechanical assembly in the Alchemical’s spine. When activated, it reduces by one the Speed of any action of Speed 4+, to a minimum of 3. This Charm may benefit any Attribute-based action for which the character has an installed Augmentation. Physical actions become highly precise and efficient, cognition occurs with the mind briefly cleansed of all distractions, and speech becomes clipped and slightly rushed as the Alchemical makes his points quickly and concisely.

Subsynaptic Accelerator

(Dexterity 5, Essence 3, 4xp):

The Charm applies a -2 Speed modifier instead (still Speed 3 minimum). A second installation of this submodule at Essence 4 allows an alternative cost of four motes and one Willpower to drop the Speed of the enhanced action to 3 outright.

Cluster Action Hyperprocessor

(Dexterity 5, Wits 5, Essence 3, 6xp):

The cost to activate this Charm is zero motes if a previous action in the same flurry already benefited from the Charm, usually allowing one instance of activation cost to reduce the Speed of the entire flurry.

Thought As Action Node

(Wits 5, Essence 4, 6xp):

For an extra one-mote surcharge, the Charm lowers the DV penalty of the supplemented action by one point.

Metacompetency Driver

(Any Physical Attribute Augmentation, Any Social Attribute Augmentation, Any Mental Attribute Augmentation, Essence 5, 2xp):

The Charm may be used to enhance the Speed of actions using any Attribute.
