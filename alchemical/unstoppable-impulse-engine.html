Unstoppable Impulse Engine

Costs: —, Mins: Strength 5, Essence 4, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Piston-Driven Megaton Hammer
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 123)

The bones of the Alchemical’s arm or leg are replaced with a mass driver assembly housed around its prerequisite Charm. When used to fire the piston, this Charm produces a sonic boom and tremendous venting of Essence around the attacking limb, making the attack unblockable. This Charm can enhance any close combat attack, any Thrown attack hurling a solid projectile (which also triples the attack’s range) or any solid projectile Archery attack provided by another Alchemical Charm.
