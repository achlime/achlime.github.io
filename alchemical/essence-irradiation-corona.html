Essence Irradiation Corona

Costs: 2m, Mins: Strength 2, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Physical Attribute Augmentation
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 124)

Four collapsible prongs are housed in one of the Exalt’s limbs. When activated, they rise up and arc forward, bathing his attacking limb or weapon in an unstable Essence field that explodes upon contact with an enemy. Any attack enhanced by this Charm converts one die of its post-soak damage into an automatic success and counts each 10 on the roll as two successes.

Motonic Reactor Discharge

(Strength 4, Essence 4, 6xp):

The Alchemical’s Essence Irradiation Corona gains the Stackable keyword, and can be activated up to (Essence / 2) times to enhance a single attack.

Optimized Trauma Upgrade

(Strength 3, 3xp):

A red jade coating applied to the Charm’s emitter arrays render its Essence field especially volatile. An attack enhanced by this submodule adds one die to its post-soak damage.

Field Stabilization Fins

(Strength 4, 3xp):

Retractable white jade fins adorn the Charm’s emitter prongs, stabilizing its Essence field. An attack enhanced by this submodule adds three dice to its raw damage.
