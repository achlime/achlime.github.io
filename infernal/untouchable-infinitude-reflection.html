Compendium Infernal Cecelyne

Untouchable Infinitude Reflection

Costs: 4m, 1wp, Mins: Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Transcendent Desert Creature
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Infernals (p. 124)

Cecelyne observes all who tread upon her until the tedium of their passage no longer holds her attention. Her contemplation of greater truths affords a measure of sanctuary for her will. Whenever an Infernal with this Charm active spends Willpower points to resist mental influence, her Dodge MDV cumulatively increases by two per point spent for the duration of the Charm. There is no upper limit on this bonus as it explicitly bypasses DV caps, but attacks taking advantage of the Imperfection of the Endless Desert bypass the Charm.
