Compendium Infernal Ebon Dragon

Wickedness Beyond Life

Costs: —, Mins: Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Witness to Darkness
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Infernals (p. 150)

The Ebon Dragon is first and foremost an enemy to life, but he regards death as an unacceptable competitor to the enslavement he brings. Purchase of this Charm expands all known Ebon Dragon Charms that specify they affect living beings, allowing them to affect creatures of death and automata.
