Compendium Infernal Kimbery

Bitter Heart Unbleeding

Costs: 3m, Mins: Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 7)
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Intolerable Burning Truths
Source: The Broken-Winged Crane (p. 42), Scroll of Errata (p. 93)

Hate shelters The Sea That Marched Against the Flame from the wretched and vile universe that wants to hurt her. Bitter Heart Unbleeding may be used to add a bonus of (total number of negative Intimacies) to the Infernal’s soak against a single instance of damage. To onlookers, it appears that the injuries luckily missed vital areas or that the warlock evaded at the last moment to suffer only grazing wounds.

An Essence 3+ repurchase grants additional powers. The Infernal can pay five extra motes to dissolve the injurious power of an attack outright (reducing raw and final damage to zero). Denying damage this way carries the Imperfection of The Sea That Marched Against the Flame, causing tongues of hissing green fog to rise from the wounds that never were.
