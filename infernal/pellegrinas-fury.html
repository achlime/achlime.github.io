Compendium Infernal Adorjan

Pellegrina’s Fury

Costs: 6m, 1wp, Mins: Essence 3, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Thousandfold Typhoon Hand
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Infernals (p. 142), Scroll of Errata (p. 92)

The Grinding Wind destroys all stone she touches, avenging an ancient slight against her mother by Pasiap during the Time of Glory. Upon activating this Charm, the character’s anima banner swells to the 11 to 15 mote level and remains so for its duration. More importantly, all stones and nonmagical objects predominantly made of stones suffer one level of lethal damage per tick that they remain within a radius of (Essence) yards, with damage manifesting as accelerated wind erosion. The damaging aura of this Charm ignores Hardness. A stone floor will still support the character’s weight while her power grinds it away, at least until that segment is actually destroyed.
