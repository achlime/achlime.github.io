Compendium Infernal Ebon Dragon

Despair-Choked Spirit Maiming

Costs: 8m, Mins: Essence 3, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Sorcerous
Duration: One day
Prerequisite charms: Ever-Hungry Shadow Affliction
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Infernals (p. 153)

From the breath of Essence to the very will that holds a psyche together, no hope escapes the Ebon Dragon’s touch. This Charm is identical to Ever-Hungry Shadow Affliction, save that it blocks the regaining of Willpower and cannot be repurchased.
