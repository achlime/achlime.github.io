Panicked Soldier Stampede

Costs: — (+1wp), Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious, War
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Post-Traumatic Brutality Roar
Source: Ink Monkeys Collection (p. 80)

Soldiers train to fight other soldiers, not Things That Must Not Be. This Charm permanently improves its prerequisite. By spending an additional Willpower to activate Post-Traumatic Brutality Roar, the scream also induces a rout check in all units successfully penalized by it. This check is made at a +2 difficulty modifier.

With Essence 6-7, the rout difficulty modifier rises to +3. It jumps to +4 at Essence 8-10.
