Spite-Fed Wounding Nimbus

Costs: —, Mins: Essence 4, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None

Prerequisite charms: Life-Blighting Emptiness Attack
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Infernals (p. 153)

The suffering of others should never be left to chance. The Infernal’s Life-Blighting Emptiness Attack no longer inflicts dice of damage. Instead, for every three dice that would be rolled, it inflicts two levels of damage instead. Any leftover dice are lost.
