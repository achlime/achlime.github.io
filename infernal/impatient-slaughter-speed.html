Impatient Slaughter Speed

Costs: —, Mins: Martial Arts 4, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Raging Behemoth Charge
Source: Ink Monkeys Collection (p. 76), Scroll of Errata (p. 188)

The Infernal Monster charges toward enemies. They are prey, not threats. This Charm upgrades Raging Behemoth Charge to add (Martial Arts) bonus dice on any roll to maintain balance or maneuver through difficult conditions. While using Raging Behemoth Charge, the Infernal also gains the following additional benefits:

  • He cannot become fatigued from exertion. Any fatigue penalties suffered at the time of activation are suspended until the Charm ends, resuming as though no time had passed.
  • He can run through muck, climb and swim toward valid opponents at the same speed as ground movement (rather than half).
  • When moving toward a valid opponent, the Infernal’s actions aren’t capped by Athletics over unstable footing or swimming in rough conditions. He effectively has whatever Athletics he needs to ignore such complications.
