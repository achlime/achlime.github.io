Interlocution Metamorphosis Prophecy

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Essence 3, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Disembodied Voice Tintinnabula
Source: The Broken-Winged Crane (p. 23)

In the perfect hierarchy, the definition of conversation varies from that of Creation. In short, when a superior speaks, an inferior has only to listen. A character invoking this Charm may target a single language which she does not speak, allowing her to communicate with all who understand the tongue in question. This Charm does not confer the ability to speak a language she does not know, but allows her to be understood by those who know the language she has subsumed.
