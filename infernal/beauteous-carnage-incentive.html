Compendium Infernal Adorjan

Beauteous Carnage Incentive

Costs: —, Mins: Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Native
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Murder is Meat
Source: The Broken-Winged Crane (p. 26), Scroll of Errata (p. 93)

With experience of the world, Adorjan’s appetite to share enlightenment only grows. This Charm acts as an additional 10 mote Peripheral Essence pool. The Infernal can convert the offensive motes granted by Murder is Meat into peripheral motes to refill the Essence pool granted by this Charm.
