Rank-Paralyzing Horror Infliction

Costs: —, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 4, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious, War
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Post-Traumatic Brutality Roar
Source: Ink Monkeys Collection (p. 80)

Fear is a cancerous malaise that grows over time, sapping competence. This Charm permanently improves its prerequisite. All units currently suffering a penalty to Drill/Morale from invocations of the Infernal’s Post-Traumatic Brutality Roar also suffer an equivalent internal penalty to all non-reflexive rolled actions using Attributes and/or Abilities.
