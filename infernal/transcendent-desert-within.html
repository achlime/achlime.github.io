Transcendent Desert Within

Costs: —, Mins: Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Native
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Holy Land Infliction
Source: The Broken-Winged Crane (p. 15)

Meditating upon the nature of desolation, the warlock solidifies the power of the Endless Desert into his own body, filling its dark inner hollows with fine-grained silver sand. This Charm acts as an additional 10 mote Peripheral Essence pool refilled with Essence generated by prayer from the character’s Cult.
