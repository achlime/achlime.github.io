Compendium Infernal Ebon Dragon

Sweet Agony Savored

Costs: —, Mins: Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Native
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Life-Blighting Emptiness Attack
Source: The Broken-Winged Crane (p. 31), Scroll of Errata (p. 31)

The banquets of gods are as dust in the Ebon Dragon’s mouth when compared to the succulent nourishment of a world in agony. This Charm acts as an additional 10-mote Peripheral Essence pool. The Infernal can convert the offensive motes granted by Life-Blighting Emptiness Attack into peripheral motes to refill the Essence pool granted by this Charm.
