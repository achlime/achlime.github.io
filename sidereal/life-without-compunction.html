Compendium Sidereal Socialize

Life Without Compunction

Costs: 5m, Mins: Socialize 4, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Emotion, Social, Virtue (Temperance)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Shun the Smiling Lady
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 149-150), Scroll of Errata (p. 67)

If a character commits a social faux pas or otherwise violates some cultural taboo, a Sidereal can use this Charm to mitigate the social backlash. She can do so either for herself, or on someone else’s behalf.

This Charm’s unnatural Emotion of acceptance applies against all witnesses with MDV less than (Exalt’s Manipulation + Socialize). Not only do people refuse to hold the infraction against the person who committed it, but they either consider the behavior acceptable under the circumstances or hail the violator as a charming rogue. Any present social group accepts the behavior as part of the community’s Policy if their leader’s MDV is less than half (Sidereal’s Manipulation + Socialize). This effect lasts for the rest of the story. Later, people regain their senses.

This Charm won’t necessarily save the perpetrator from the legal repercussions of breaking laws, only from the way other people might regard him. This Charm permits a special form of dodging in social combat: when the character comes under social attack, she says something outrageously offensive about her attacker, and activates this Charm. With one success, she distracts onlookers from the social attack and doesn’t have to answer her attacker’s argument. With three, she pulls the sympathy of any onlookers to her side.
