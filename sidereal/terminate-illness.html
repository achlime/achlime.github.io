Compendium Sidereal Medicine

Terminate Illness

Costs: 5m, Mins: Medicine 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Touch, Virtue (Conviction)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 185), Scroll of Errata (p. 80)

The Sidereal can use this Charm when a person is suffering from some normal disease—i.e., not a Charm’s Sickness effect or the effects of an infectious martial art (from Scroll of the Monk, pp. 153–154). The Sidereal makes the Inferior Sign of the Corpse over the sick person, targeting the least god of that person’s illness. No roll is needed to end mundane diseases, otherwise the Sidereal’s player rolls (Intelligence + Medicine + Essence) against the disease’s listed Difficulty to Treat. If the roll succeeds, the illness’s least god relinquishes its hold on the victim, and the victim begins to recover.

With Essence 3+, the Sidereal can use this Charm to cure even magical Sickness. Doing so requires a roll, and extends the time needed to activate this Charm to one hour. Magical diseases without a Virulence rating cannot be cured with this Charm.
