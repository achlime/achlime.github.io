Compendium Sidereal Resistance

Ox-Body Technique

Costs: —, Mins: Resistance 1, Essence 1, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Stackable
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 127)

Just as all Exalted, Sidereals gain extra health levels with each purchase of this Charm. Each health level gained thus helps a Sidereal operate at maximum efficiency in spite of his wounds for just a little bit longer. A player may purchase this Charm for his character up to once per dot of the Resistance Ability his character possesses. Each purchase grants the character one additional -0 health level.
