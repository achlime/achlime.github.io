Compendium Sidereal Ride

Glory Path

Costs: 8m, Mins: Ride 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple (Speed 6, DV -2)
Keywords: Combo-Basic, Obvious
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Yellow Path
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 132), Scroll of Errata (p. 59)

As long as this Charm as active, the Sidereal’s mount can Dash reflexively and without the risk of fatigue. Also, the animal can gallop through, up or over natural obstacles such as dense forests, crowds, cliffs, open water or even gaping chasms as easily as it can move across flat, open terrain. Unnatural obstacles, such as human-made walls or sorcerous barricades, still impede progress as normal.
