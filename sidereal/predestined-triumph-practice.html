Compendium Sidereal War

Predestined Triumph Practice

Costs: —, Mins: War 4, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Virtue (Valor), War
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Auspicious Recruitment Drive
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 160), Scroll of Errata (p. 70)

Whether a Sidereal leads from the front or coaches from the rear, he can use this Charm to help lead an army to victory. This Charm affects any unit that includes the Sidereal as a special character. The affected unit adds the Sidereal’s Essence to the unit’s Morale, while opposing forces suffer a -1 external penalty to their attacks and Defense Values. The Virtue keyword allows the Exalt to channel Valor on rolls for the unit to resist Exhaustion (Exalted, pp. 168-169), and to succeed on their Morale rolls automatically with 0 extra successes.
