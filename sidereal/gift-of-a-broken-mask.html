Compendium Sidereal Stealth

Gift of a Broken Mask

Costs: 6m, 1wp, Mins: Stealth 2, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-Basic, Shaping, Touch
Duration: One week
Prerequisite charms: Any Stealth Excellency, Soft Presence Practice
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 171-172), Scroll of Errata (p. 74)

The Sidereals can grant other people their own concealment from the world, as a blessing to assist them… or a curse. The Vizier must touch her target; the Vizier’s player rolls (Wits + Stealth) against the target’s Dodge MDV. For every extra success, minimum 1, the target is subjected to a day of Arcane Fate, with all the advantages and inconveniences that Sidereals know so well. On the other hand, he becomes immune to the Arcane Fates of others; the Sidereals’ gift makes the target an honorary member of the Five-Score Fellowship while the Charm lasts. The target’s Essence cannot exceed the Sidereal’s own. The Sidereal can rescind the Arcane Fate at any time.

Sidereals with Essence 4+ may add +1wp to the Cost of this Charm to target characters up to (Essence) yards away, or to target a social group with Magnitude no greater than her Essence. There is no special requirement that the Exalt or an ally lead the group to target it with this Charm.

When targetting a social group, the difficulty becomes the sum of the unit’s Magnitude and the highest Essence of any member of the group, other than the Sidereal himself. As with a single target, no member’s Essence score may exceed the Sidereal’s own; the unit’s Magnitude cannot exceed his Stealth. While the Charm is active, all activities of the social group as a whole are easily overlooked, and other people tend to ignore the unit’s existence. In mass social combat, apply an internal penalty of -3 to the defender’s side of the contested (Manipulation + Socialize) vs. (Wits + Awareness) roll to establish surprise and make unexpected social attacks.
