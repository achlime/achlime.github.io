Compendium Sidereal Occult

Mark of Exaltation

Costs: 3m, Mins: Occult 2, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, Virtue (Compassion)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 169), Scroll of Errata (p. 74)

The Sidereal makes of his caste mark a lantern that can reveal unmanifested spirits. The mark glows with the color associated with his caste and is visible to spirits and to Chosen of his caste. Anyone who can see the light can also see any unmanifested spirits within (Essence x 50) yards on whom the light shines. The light also adds one to the Sidereal’s effective Appearance rating when interacting with spirits in social combat. This Charm is Obvious only to characters capable of seeing the Mark’s light.
