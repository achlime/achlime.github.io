Compendium Sidereal Performance

Heart-Brightening Presentation Style

Costs: —, Mins: Performance 2, Essence 1, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Virtue (Compassion)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 146), Scroll of Errata (p. 65)

The Sidereal’s skill at poetry and song infuses his speech at all times. The character can apply any Bureaucracy, Performance, Presence or Socialize Excellency to any dice pool involving those four Abilities. The normal limits for raising dice pools through Charms still apply.

In addition, this Charm allows the Exalt to channel Compassion on any rolls enhanced by those Excellencies. The first time she does so in a scene costs 0wp.
