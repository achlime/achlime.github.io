Compendium Sidereal Stealth

Blinding the Boar

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Stealth 2, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Illusion, Virtue (Valor)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 171-172), Scroll of Errata (p. 75)

The Sidereal cloaks herself in the reflections of a thousand possible futures, making it almost impossible to focus on the “her” that exists in the present. The character is obviously present and real, perceptible by sight and touch, but determining any specific detail about her proves impossible. Where exactly she stands, what she wears, what she says, her appearance, even her gender.... None of these qualities makes an impression on an observer’s mind.

This Charm targets an individual up to (Essence) yards away. There is no activation roll; Blinding the Boar is a form of unblockable and undodgeable unnatural Illusion. It may be ignored for 3wp per action. The target finds it impossible to recognize the Sidereal, and also faces a -3 external penalty on all actions targeting the Exalt.
