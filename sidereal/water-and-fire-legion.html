Compendium Sidereal Resistance

Water and Fire Legion

Costs: —, Mins: Resistance 4, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Maiden, Servitude
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Water and Fire Treaty
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 128), Scroll of Errata (p. 58)

Sacrifice in Creation is a trade of pain and love. Should the Sidereal invoke Water and Fire Treaty by touching a living elemental, the spirit is spurred to become a guardian of something the Exalt holds dear. The Exalt rolls (Charisma + Resistance) against the target’s Dodge MDV, and if successful binds them per the spell Summon Elemental (Exalted, p. 254), except that the Sidereal can only bind the elemental to protect the subject of one of the Exalt’s Intimacies. The chosen subject must be a discrete thing, including an object, a group of objects, a structure, a character, or a social group. Exalts using this Charm to bind elementals of the kind associated with their Maiden and Caste gain two automatic successes on their social attack roll.
