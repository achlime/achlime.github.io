Compendium Sidereal Archery

Any Direction Arrow

Costs: 2m, Mins: Archery 1, Essence 1, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 151), Scroll of Errata (p. 67)

Weaving her arrows together with strands of martial destiny, the Sidereal can loose them all in a single shot. Activating this Charm, the Sidereal may nock and release up to (Dexterity) arrows as part of a single Archery attack. The base damage of each individual arrow is added to the raw damage of the attack, and any tags or special qualities possessed by an arrow are applied to the whole attack.
