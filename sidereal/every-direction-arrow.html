Compendium Sidereal Archery

Every Direction Arrow

Costs: 3m per extra arrow, Mins: Archery 4, Essence 2, Type: Simple (Speed 5, DV -2)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Direction Arrow
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 151)

The Sidereal loads several arrows onto his string and fires them all at once. The arrows initially scatter but converge swiftly on a single target, striking him simultaneously. The Sidereal’s player rolls only a single attack, but each arrow applies its damage separately. The Exalt can fire a maximum number of projectiles in a single attack equal to his Essence.
