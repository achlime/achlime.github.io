Compendium Sidereal Occult

Incite Decorum

Costs: 2m, Mins: Occult 2, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Compulsion
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 169-170), Scroll of Errata (p. 74)

A Sidereal can invoke his Maiden’s authority to facilitate polite dealings with any spirits officially employed by Heaven. Any spirit whose Essence is no higher than 8 must remain calm, reasonable and peaceful in the Vizier’s presence. The spirit must spend two Willpower points to initiate violence against the Seer, and any Charm the spirit uses against the character costs one additional Willpower. (This includes Charms that normally do not cost Willpower at all.) The effect ends automatically if the Sidereal opens hostilities himself.
