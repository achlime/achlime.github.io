Compendium Sidereal Archery

Opportune Shot

Costs: 2m per reduction, Mins: Archery 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 151-152)

The character spots the moment in which fate intends for him to take his shot, and he rushes to accommodate. The Sidereal reduces the Speed of an Archery-based attack by one tick for every two motes spent to activate the Charm. Opportune Shot and other effects can reduce the attack’s final Speed to a minimum of 3.
