Compendium Sidereal War

Essence-Draining Battle Pattern

Costs: 5m, Mins: War 5, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Overdrive, War
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Predestined Triumph Practice
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 161), Scroll of Errata (p. 71)

Through the brilliant planning of the Sidereal Exalted, the stymied efforts of foemen become the raw vigor of the Vizier’s allies. A Sidereal may activate this Charm to enhance a mass combat unit in which he is a leader or special character. On each of the unit’s action ticks, the leader and every special character in the unit gains one offensive mote in their Overdrive pools; those characters who possess no Overdrive pool gain a temporary five-mote pool while this Charm is active, which begins empty (characters who already possess Overdrive pools do not raise the size of their pool).

A unit can benefit from only one Battle Pattern Charm at a time.
