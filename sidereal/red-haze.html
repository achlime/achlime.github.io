Compendium Sidereal War

Red Haze

Costs: —, Mins: War 3, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Holy, Stackable, War
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 158-159), Scroll of Errata (p. 70)

Crimson stardust, visible only to Sidereals or characters with supernatural senses, wafts down from the sky onto a unit of soldiers. This Charm applies automatically to units whose Magnitude cannot exceed the Sidereal’s Essence, and which include the Sidereal as a special character. Every combatant, man, beast or spirit, gains a primal opposition to entities from beyond Creation. If the anointed soldiers face demons, the dead, the Fair Folk or other creatures of darkness during the five-day duration, the soldiers enjoy a +1 bonus to their Drill rating.

A single Sidereal can affect a given unit only once at a time, but several Sidereals working together can affect the same unit simultaneously, increasing the bonus. The maximum bonus equals the highest Essence rating among the Sidereals activating the Charm simultaneously. The maximum Magnitude of a unit that can be affected by multiple Exalts is equal to the lowest Essence amongst them, plus one for each additional Seer.
