Unweaving Method

Costs: 5m, 1wp, 1lhl, Mins: Occult 4, Essence 3, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, Shaping
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Telltale Symphony, Incite Decorum
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Sidereals (p. 170), Scroll of Errata (p. 74)

Lending a sense of imminent doom to her own destiny, the Sidereal lashes out with the threads of her fate like a whip, ensnaring a target with tragedy and corruption. This is a (Dexterity + Occult) attack, with Accuracy +0, Damage 4A/2, Rate 1, Range (Exalt’s Essence x 10), Tags: O. It cannot be blocked or dodged, but it also cannot target creatures of death, such as Abyssal Exalted, ghosts and ghost-blooded.
