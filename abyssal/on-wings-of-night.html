Compendium Abyssal Athletics

On Wings of Night

Costs: 4m, 1wp, Mins: Athletics 5, Essence 4, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Eagle-Wing Style)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Crouching Gargoyle Stance, Mist Over Ice
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 165)

The Abyssal’s anima billows out ominously like a tempest-blown cape or the wings of a bat, lifting him to prowl the air. Unlike its Solar Mirror, this Charm does not require an extended hand, but it automatically deactivates and cannot be activated while the light of Creation’s sun shines on the deathknight.
