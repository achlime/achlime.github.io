Compendium Abyssal Athletics

Effortless Unnatural Grace

Costs: — (1m), Mins: Athletics 5, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Mirror (Perfect Poise Stance)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Raiton’s Nimble Perch
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 165), Scroll of Errata (p. 88)

Lesser Abyssals might steady themselves with Essence for a time, only to fall flat on their faces at a key moment in combat when they cannot afford to waste a Charm activation on the effect. The Abyssal can pay one mote while Raiton’s Nimble Perch is active to perfectly negate any knockback applied against him. More importantly, whenever the Abyssal has Raiton’s Nimble Perch active, she can spend one mote to negate all damage from falling and automatically succeeds on any attempt to arrest a fall by grabbing onto something (unless there is absolutely nothing available to grab).

New Solar Charm: Perfect Poise Stance (Prerequisites: Graceful Crane Stance). This Charm functions identically to Effortless Unnatural Grace.
