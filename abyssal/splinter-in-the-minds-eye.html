Splinter in the Mind’s Eye

Costs: 10m, 1wp, Mins: Stealth 5, Essence 3, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Vanishing from Mind’s Eye Method)
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Atrocity Without Witness
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 172)

Abyssals are nameless world-slaying weapons who forsake any right to be remembered, but such blasphemous anonymity has its perks. Note that this Charm cannot obscure memories of the character before his Exaltation, as that was effectively another person.
