Compendium Abyssal Melee

Five Shadow Feint

Costs: 2m per DV, Mins: Melee 2, Essence 1, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Melee Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 129)

The Abyssal’s weapon spins in a flicker of shadowy images, confusing her foe. For every two motes the Exalt spends on Five Shadow Feint, her target’s DV against the attack drops by one.
