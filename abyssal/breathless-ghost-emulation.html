Breathless Ghost Emulation

Costs: 4m, 1wp, Mins: Resistance 5, Essence 3, Type: Simple
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: One day
Prerequisite charms: Corpse Needs No Food
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 148)

The dead breathe only prayers. Abyssals with this Charm active need not even bother with that, breathing only when they wish to do so (such as to facilitate speech) without suffering asphyxiation.
