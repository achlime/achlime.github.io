Death-Deflecting Technique

Costs: 8m, Mins: Melee 4, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 2)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Heavenly Guardian Defense), Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Vengeful Riposte
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 131), Scroll of Errata (p. 86)

Whether the arrows of man or the spear of the Unconquered Sun, no force may strike an armed Abyssal with this Charm. Note that this Charm does differ slightly from its Solar counterpart in its Essence cost and bearing the Spectral keyword.
