Compendium Abyssal Presence

Lurking Malice Insinuation

Costs: —, Mins: Presence 5, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Mirror (Minds Yield to Glory)
Duration: N/A
Prerequisite charms: Poisoning the Will
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 143)

This Charm expands its prerequisite, increasing its duration to one month. If the Abyssal has Essence 4+, he can make the cost five Willpower to resist when the first opportunity to fulfill the order arises (in lieu of one Willpower per day), after which the Charm ends. Finally, if the deathknight has Essence 5+, the mote cost to activate Poisoning the Will is only the target’s Essence rating instead of 10m.

New Solar Charm: Minds Yield to Glory (Prerequisites: Hypnotic Tongue Technique). This Charm improves Hypnotic Tongue Technique but otherwise works the same as Lurking Malice Insinuation.
