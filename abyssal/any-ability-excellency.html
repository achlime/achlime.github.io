Compendium Abyssal

Any (Ability) Excellency

Source: Core Rulebook (p. 183-185)

This prerequisite is satisfied if the Abyssal knows any of the alternative Charms listed. The alternative charm must be known for the appropriate Ability.

Possible alternatives:
  • First (Ability) Excellency
  • Second (Ability) Excellency
  • Third (Ability) Excellency
