Vengeful Riposte

Costs: 3m, Mins: Melee 4, Essence 1, Type: Reflexive (Step 9)
Keywords: Combo-Basic, Counterattack, Martial-ready (Archery, Martial Arts, Thrown), Mirror (Solar Counterattack)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Elegant Flowing Deflection
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 130), Scroll of Errata (p. 86)

The Abyssal holds his weapon loosely at his side, his monstrous arrogance practically inviting opponents to strike him, while promising horrific retribution on anyone foolish enough to take the dare. The Charm does not defend the deathknight in any way, but he can strike back with his weapon at his attacker.
