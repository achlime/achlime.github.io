Compendium Abyssal Linguistics

Insidious Undertone Trick

Costs: 6m, Mins: Linguistics 4, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Letter-Within-a-Letter Technique)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Screaming in Silence
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 175-176)

A deathknight with this Charm can hide one statement inside another so subtly that bystanders don’t realize what was actually said… just like the Whispers of the Neverborn permeate the Underworld, unheard. Unlike its Solar Mirror, this Charm modifies speech rather than writing, so the Abyssal may make any single Manipulation-based social attack using any Ability as part of activating the Charm. Doing so adds the Social keyword and changes the Charm’s type to Simple (Speed 6 in long ticks), though.
