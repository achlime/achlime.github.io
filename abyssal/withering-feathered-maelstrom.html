Withering Feathered Maelstrom

Costs: 1m or 2m per attack, Mins: Archery 3, Essence 2, Type: Extra Action
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Trance of Unhesitating Speed), Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 123), Scroll of Errata (p. 85)
