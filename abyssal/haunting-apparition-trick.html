Compendium Abyssal Performance

Haunting Apparition Trick

Costs: —, Mins: Performance 3, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Mirror (Phantom-Conjuring Performance), Obvious
Duration: Variable
Prerequisite charms: Any Performance Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 141), Scroll of Errata (p. 87), Scroll of Errata (p. 25)

Strange and unnatural phenomena manifest to accentuate an Abyssal’s arts. Examples include chill winds, blood dripping down walls, moving shadows and tongues of foxfire luminescence that burn without source.

At Performance and Essence 4+ a Abyssal with this Charm may personalize the expressions of his iconic anima displays, changing its appearance each time it flares up.
