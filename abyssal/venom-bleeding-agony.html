Compendium Abyssal Medicine

Venom-Bleeding Agony

Costs: 1m, Mins: Medicine 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Mirror (Body-Purifying Admonitions), Touch
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Pitiless Triage Judgment
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 160)

With a touch, an Abyssal can force a patient’s body to hemorrhage a target Poison effect as a black tarry liquid oozing from all facial orifices. Unlike its Solar Mirror, this Charm takes effect immediately, but the patient suffers one level of unsoakable aggravated damage as the toxin erupts from her face. Abyssals may treat themselves with this Charm.
