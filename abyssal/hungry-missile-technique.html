Compendium Abyssal Thrown

Hungry Missile Technique

Costs: 1m, Mins: Thrown 2, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 2)
Keywords: Merged (Blade-Summoning Gesture), Mirror (Call the Blade (Thrown)), Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Improvised Assassin’s Trick
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 132), Scroll of Errata (p. 87)

The weapons of the Abyssals are as bloodthirsty as their masters, eager to fly back to waiting hands in anticipation of burying themselves in another victim. The Abyssal may reflexively spend one mote to activate this Charm as described. He may only call his knife from up to (Essence x 2) yards away while in battle, however.
