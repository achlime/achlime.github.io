Blade-Summoning Gesture

Costs: — (1m), Mins: Melee 2, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Merged (Hungry Missile Technique), Mirror (Call the Blade (Melee)), Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 129), Scroll of Errata (p. 85)

The weapons of the Abyssal Exalted fear their masters and swiftly leap to their grasp when bidden by an imperiously outstretched hand. The Abyssal may reflexively spend one mote to activate this Charm. He may only call his blade from up to (Essence x 2) yards away while in battle, however.
