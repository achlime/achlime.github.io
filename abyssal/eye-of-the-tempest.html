Compendium Abyssal Melee

Eye of the Tempest

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Melee 5, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Martial (Martial Arts), Mirror (Fivefold Bulwark Stance)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Vengeful Riposte
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 130), Scroll of Errata (p. 86)

The Abyssal is a figure of transcendent stillness and patience within the bloody fury of combat, systematically turning aside blows without the slightest wasted motion. This Charm only applies its benefits to the Exalt’s Melee-derived Parry DV.
