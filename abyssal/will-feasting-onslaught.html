Will-Feasting Onslaught

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Lore 3, Essence 1, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Will-Bolstering Method), Obvious, Touch
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Essence-Draining Touch
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 156)

The Abyssal gorges her heart with stolen resolve. This Charm reverses its Solar Mirror, transferring Willpower points from the target to the deathknight. Like its prerequisite, the target must be willing, or at least not trying to resist.
