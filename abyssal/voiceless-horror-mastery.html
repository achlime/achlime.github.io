Compendium Abyssal Linguistics

Voiceless Horror Mastery

Costs: —, Mins: Linguistics 4, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Screaming in Silence
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 176)

This Charm enhances its prerequisite such that the Abyssal can communicate with any perceived creature of Death within (Linguistics) miles. If she has used Screaming in Silence on a particular creature of Death before, she does not even need to perceive that target to initiate contact, though her player must roll as described for that Charm. Contact fails if that target is outside range or the roll fails. The Abyssal may still use Screaming in Silence normally to contact beings who are not creatures of Death.
