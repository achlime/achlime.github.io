Compendium Abyssal Melee

Unfurling Iron Lotus

Costs: 3m, Mins: Melee 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple (Speed 5, DV -1)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Peony Blossom Attack), Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Five Shadow Feint
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 129), Scroll of Errata (p. 85), Scroll of Errata (p. 22)

The Abyssal spins and thrusts in a cascade of blows, trailing wispy afterimages that fleetingly resemble the petals of a blooming flower before the victim’s screams break the serenity of the display. The Abyssal makes a standard Melee attack at his full dice pool, and applies it to all enemies within three yards; each target resolves defense and damage separately.
