Compendium Abyssal Craft

World-Slaying Arsenal Epiphany

Costs: —, Mins: Craft 5, Essence 5, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Avatar (3), Mirror (Wonder-Forging Genius)
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Craft Essence Flow, Frenzied Forge Within
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 153)

Inspired by the alien and genocidal genius of the Neverborn, the most depraved Abyssal engineers invent weapons that would take lesser Exalted centuries to conceive. Once a deathknight purchases this Charm, the Craft, Lore, Medicine and Occult minimums required to design, build and repair destructive artifacts decrease by one (to a minimum of 1). The Exalt receives the same benefits toward creating Abyssal manses. For the purposes of this Charm, a destructive artifact must meet the same criteria as a destructive item defined by Frenzied Forge Within. An Abyssal may buy this Charm no more than twice.
