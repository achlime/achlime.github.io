Compendium Abyssal Integrity

Soulsteel Reaper Spirit

Costs: —, Mins: Integrity 1, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Dusk
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Ink Monkeys Collection (p. 45)

Wielding the power of soulsteel, a deathknight may draw Essence from his victims. This Charm enhances that proclivity. If the Exalt has an Overdrive pool, he may select to divert soulsteel-drained motes to his Overdrive pool. If the deathknight selects to place all the motes reaped from a single attack into his Overdrive pool, he gains an additional offensive mote.
