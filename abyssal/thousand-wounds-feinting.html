Compendium Abyssal Melee

Thousand Wounds Feinting

Costs: —, Mins: Melee 5, Essence 4, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Mirror (Invincible Fury of the Dawn)
Duration: N/A
Prerequisite charms: Time-Scything Technique
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 129), Scroll of Errata (p. 85)

The Abyssal learns to strike with impossible speed, fulfilling the Charm’s name with blurs of violence that leave bewildered victims covered in countless shallow cuts or bruises. Only a handful of blows have any real force to them, but these are usually sufficient.

This Charm modifies Time-Scything Technique by granting it two additional attacks, as well as two additional maximum attacks (for a total of Dexterity + 3 attacks).

In addition, a character who uses this Charm with a successful flurry may incidentally arrange the wounds inflicted into artful designs or calligraphy to add literal insult to injury. At the Storyteller’s option, Solars can do the same with Invincible Fury of the Dawn. If the player can think of something particularly witty or obnoxious for the character to inscribe in blood, the Storyteller might consider granting a stunt bonus.
