Honey-Tongued Serpent Attack

Costs: 3m, 1wp, Mins: Socialize 5, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Heartfelt Honorific Opportunity), Social
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Exquisite Etiquette Style
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 181), Scroll of Errata (p. 88)

The Abyssal’s courtesies catch opponents off guard. This Charm may enhance any Manipulation-based social attack, making the attack unblockable and undodgeable. It is explicitly permitted to supplement actions using other Abilities.

New Solar Charm: Heartfelt Honorific Opportunity (Prerequisites: Mastery of Small Manners). Apart from its prerequisite, this Charm functions identically to its Mirror.
