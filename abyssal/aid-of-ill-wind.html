Aid of Ill Wind

Costs: 2m, Mins: Thrown 2, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Triple-Distance Attack Technique)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 132), Scroll of Errata (p. 87)

Chill gusts from the Underworld carry the Abyssal’s weapon to his prey. These winds dissipate on impact, leaving wounds that smell faintly of funereal incense or wilting flowers.
