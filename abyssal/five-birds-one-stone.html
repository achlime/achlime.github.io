Compendium Abyssal Thrown

Five Birds, One Stone

Costs: 2m per attack, Mins: Thrown 5, Essence 2, Type: Extra Action
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Wind Full of Knives), Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Aid of Ill Wind
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 132)

Once released, the Abyssal’s weapon scythes from target to target in a path of destruction. This Charm is a magical flurry of up to (Thrown) attacks with no multiple-action penalties, all of which must be aimed at targets within the weapon’s range. Because the attacks all use a single Thrown weapon, the flurry ignores the weapon’s Rate and has a DV penalty of the highest penalty for any attack. This Charm is particularly spectacular in conjunction with Crypt Bolt Attack (see p. 134), firing chain lightning or summoning tentacles of obscene energy that twist and arc from victim to victim.

New Solar Charm: Wind Full of Knives (Prerequisites: Triple-Distance Attack Technique). The radiance of the sun glows around the Lawgiver’s weapon as he prepares to throw it. The shining weapon whirls from target to target, cutting them down with Solar fury.
