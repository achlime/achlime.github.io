Compendium Abyssal Archery

Flawless Archer Discipline

Costs: 1m, 1wp, Mins: Archery 5, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Accuracy Without Distance), Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Pulse of the Prey
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 122)

Nothing can save those whom an Abyssal archer has chosen for death. As the arrow leaves her bow, a shrieking spectral aura guides her shaft inexorably to its target. As with the Solar Charm, the player rolls the attack but only to see if there are sufficient successes to add to damage.
