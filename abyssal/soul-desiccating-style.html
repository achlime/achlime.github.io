Soul-Desiccating Style

Costs: 6m, Mins: Performance 4, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Emotion, Mirror (Heart-Compelling Method), Social
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Any Performance Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 142)

Deathknights bring out the worst feelings in their audiences: fear, hate, shame and so on. Unlike its Solar Mirror, this Charm can instill only negative emotions, but resisting it for the scene costs three Willpower.
