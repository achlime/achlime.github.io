Compendium Abyssal Thrown

Crypt Bolt Attack

Costs: 4m, Mins: Thrown 4, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive (Step 1)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (Spirit Weapons), Obvious
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Improvised Assassin’s Trick
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 134), Scroll of Errata (p. 87)

Unnatural entropic and corpse-element energies crackle like ball lightning around one or both hands of the Abyssal’s hands. Like an iconic anima, each user of this Charm creates a unique and easily recognizable manifestation of Essence. If an Abyssal makes an unarmed punch with a hand wreathed in Crypt Bolt Attack, the punch inflicts lethal damage. It is far more effective for him to throw part of this energy as a blast or beam, using the nimbus as a source of infinite ammunition while the Charm lasts.

These blasts have the following traits: Speed 5, Accuracy +0, Rate 2 (one per hand), Range (Thrown x 10 yards) and inflict a base damage of 6L without adding Strength, they also have the Overwhelming tag, with a minimum damage of 2. This Charm inflicts damage through accelerated decay, corrosion or some other consumptive principle. There is no mistaking these wounds for natural injuries.

Using a hand holding a Crypt Bolt Attack for anything other than throwing blasts, punching or making unarmed parries dissipates the energies, requiring the Abyssal to activate the Charm again.
