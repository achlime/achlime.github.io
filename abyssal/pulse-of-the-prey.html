Compendium Abyssal Archery

Pulse of the Prey

Costs: 3m or 5m, Mins: Archery 4, Essence 1, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Mirror (There is No Wind)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Archery Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 122)

No distraction can keep an Abyssal from his quarry. His attacks unnaturally twist in flight with a predatory hunger of their own to find their mark, negating all penalties except wound and multiple-action penalties.
