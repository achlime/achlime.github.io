Elemental Empowerment Meditation

Costs: —, Mins: Lore 4, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Elemental, Native
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Elemental Concentration Trance
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Dragon-Blooded (p. 133), Scroll of Errata (p. 31)

The blessings of the Terrestrial Exaltation allow the Dragon-Blooded to draw upon the might of their progenitors, the Elemental Dragons, evoking their power from the land itself. A Dragon-Blood may activate his anima effect for zero motes while he is in direct contact with some potent expression of his Aspect element—a windstorm, a bonfire, the ocean, and so on. In addition, the Dragon-Blood multiplies the rate at which he naturally recovers motes by his (Essence) while resting or meditating in a landscape that is associated with his Aspect’s element.
