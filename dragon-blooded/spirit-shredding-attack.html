Spirit-Shredding Attack

Costs: 4m, Mins: Occult 5, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Leader (1)
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Spirit-Grounding Shout
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Dragon-Blooded (p. 135), Scroll of Errata (p. 32)

This charm is flagged because of:

  • Unclear errata: Errata adds Leader keyword without Essence rating, assuming Essence 1

This weapon in the Dragon-Blooded arsenal is especially hated and feared by the spirit hierarchies and is a significant explanation of how the Immaculate Order was finally able to bring the spirits of the Blessed Isle to heel. The Dragon-Blood must first make a successful attack against a spirit, either while the spirit is materialized or with some weapon or attack capable of affecting dematerialized spirits. The attack inflicts its normal damage. In addition, the attacker’s player reflexively rolls (the character’s Willpower + Essence) against a difficulty equal to the spirit’s Essence. Each threshold success on the activation roll strips away (Exalt’s Essence rating)m from the spirit as a Shaping effect. The character does not steal the Essence, it dissipates into the air. If the spirit is destroyed by this Charm, that spirit is irrevocably gone. The imposition of permanent death with a killing blow is not Shaping. Leader applies as per Spirit-Chaining Strike.
