Compendium Dragon-Blooded Lore

Elemental Concentration Trance

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Lore 2, Essence 1, Type: Simple
Keywords: None
Duration: One day
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Dragon-Blooded (p. 132), Scroll of Errata (p. 31)

The simplest of Lore techniques exercised by the savants of Air, this Charm allows a character to meditate on her aligned element to temporarily augment her capacity to absorb knowledge. Provided that the Exalt has all the necessary research material handy, she can absorb a week’s worth of study in a single day. This Charm can accelerate training times for any Charm that shares the Terrestrial’s aspect or non-magical trait. Charms to learn sorcery or necromancy are never eligible, regardless of aspect. The Charm may also be used to speed research or other actions to gain information from available sources. She can continue to use this Charm on subsequent days, but if she uses it more days in a row than a number equal to her Lore trait, she suffers one level of unsoakable lethal damage for each extra day. The Exalt cannot heal this damage while under the effects of this Charm.
