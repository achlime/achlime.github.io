Compendium Dragon-Blooded Linguistics

Language-Learning Ritual

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Linguistics 2, Essence 1, Type: Simple
Keywords: Stackable
Duration: One week
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Dragon-Blooded (p. 129), Scroll of Errata (p. 30)

The Children of Mela often serve as the diplomatic corps of the Dragon-Blooded Host, and while fluency in foreign tongues often requires years of study, this Charm can reduce the necessary time to just an afternoon. The Dragon-Blood must spend at least one hour meditating and listening to a foreign language being spoken. He can then “absorb” that language into himself, becoming completely fluent in both conversation and literacy. The Charm lasts for one week, at which time the knowledge gained fades completely. Each commitment sustains fluency in a different language (written fluency requires Lore 1).
