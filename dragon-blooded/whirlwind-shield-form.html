Compendium Dragon-Blooded Thrown

Whirlwind Shield Form

Costs: 3m + 1m per ally protected, Mins: Thrown 3, Essence 2, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK, Leader (1)
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Any Thrown Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Dragon-Blooded (p. 139), Scroll of Errata (p. 33)

This charm is flagged because of:

  • Unclear errata: Errata adds Leader keyword without Essence rating, assuming Essence 1

The flawless skill of the Dragon-Blooded can even allow them to knock arrows and daggers from the air with a well-placed throw. The Exalt using this Charm can deflect ranged attacks aimed at himself or at allies at a cost of three motes plus one mote per each ally protected. While the Charm is in effect, apply an external penalty of half the Exalt’s Essence rating against all ranged attacks made against him or protected allies within range. The Essence spent is committed for the duration of the Charm. This Charm does not require any actual weapons. Instead, the Whirlwind Shield is formed from the air itself, and any allies who are to be protected must be within (the Exalt’s Essence) yards in order for the Dragon-Blood to shield them.

The Leader use shields dots of Magnitude as though individuals to a maximum of (Essence - 1) dots; all dots must be protected for any benefit.
