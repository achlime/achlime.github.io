Compendium Lunar Stamina

Scorpion and Toad Assumption

Costs: 3m, Mins: Stamina 3, Essence 3, Type: Simple (dramatic action)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Scorpion and Toad Absolution
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 161)

This Charm is a dramatic action to treat a target for poison or disease. The Lunar Exalt spends at least five minutes preparing the body, channeling the foreign substance within the target toward a specific place, where she then cuts the body and sucks out the tainted blood. This taste of the target’s blood exposes the character to the disease or poison that plagues the target, which she then must deal with on her own.
