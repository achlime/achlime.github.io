Compendium Lunar Dexterity

Becoming Water’s Envy

Costs: —, Mins: Dexterity 5, Essence 6, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Fury-OK
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Flowing Body Evasion
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 69), Scroll of Errata (p. 174)

This Charm enhances its prerequisite, giving it the Fury-OK keyword. Activating Flowing Body Evasion with Relentless Lunar Fury allows the Lunar to automatically dodge one attack (of which she is aware) each action. Additional unbeatable dodges in a single action require the Lunar to activate Flowing Body Evasion separately. The character suffers a Flaw of Invulnerability applicable to Flowing Body Evasion for the duration of Relentless Lunar Fury.
