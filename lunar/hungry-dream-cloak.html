Compendium Lunar Knacks

Hungry Dream Cloak

Prerequisites: Luna’s Hidden Face, Essence 4, Wits 4
Source: Glories of the Most High — Luna (p. 34)

This difficult trick, popular among the Swords of Luna, permits a Lunar to drink in the woven lies the raksha use for blood and treat them as true. Fair Folk become valid targets for the sacred hunt, with the same default time as for a human. The Lunar does not gain access to Charms or other raksha powers.
