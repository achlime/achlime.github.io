Compendium Lunar Knacks

Emerald Grasshopper Form

Prerequisites: Humble Mouse Shape, Essence 3, Stamina 4
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 131)

A character with this Knack can become the literal fly on the wall. Creatures as small as the typical housefly become valid targets for the blood hunt (though some Lunars feel a bit silly spending six hours stalking an insect).
