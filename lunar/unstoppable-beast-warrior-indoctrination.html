Unstoppable Beast-Warrior Indoctrination

Costs: —, Mins: Charisma 7, Essence 6, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Lesson of the Winter Wolf, Loyal Beast-Friend Enlightenment
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 72)

Herds and packs serve the Stewards without hesitation. This Charm permanently enhances Wolf Pack Training Technique. When using Wolf Pack Training Technique on a military unit of animals, the Lunar makes them into such unified armies that they achieve Might (Essence ÷ 2) while under his command.
