Compendium Lunar Strength

Deadly Mantis Practice

Costs: 4m, Mins: Strength 4, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Molted Feather-Weight Technique
Source: Glories of the Most High — Luna (p. 22)

Concentrating and sustaining her might, the Lunar learns to channel the totality of her body’s strength into each limb. While this Charm is in effect, the character may wield weapons with the 2 tag using only one hand with no penalty.

Solars possess a similar Charm called Dual Slaying Posture. Its prerequisites are Melee 4, Essence 2 and One Weapon, Two Blows. It costs only three motes.
