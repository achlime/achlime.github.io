Compendium Lunar Stamina

Scorpion and Toad Absolution

Costs: 2m, Mins: Stamina 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Stamina Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 161)

Stewards are masters of their bodies and difficult to kill by treachery. Characters with this Charm may immunize their bodies against any poison or disease they experience. Activating Scorpion and Toad Assumption while suffering the effects, the character’s player adds three automatic successes to all (Stamina + Resistance) rolls to endure the effects of that disease or poison.
