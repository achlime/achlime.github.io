Compendium Lunar Knacks

Assimilation of the Prey

Prerequisites: Body of Acid, Quicksilver Form
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 204)

The chimera can make its body flow over and around its chosen prey, totally engulfing the victim. Once trapped within the chimera’s body, the victim suffers six lethal damage every five ticks. This damage bypasses armor, since the chimera’s internal digestive organs can flow around such protective gear. Victims who die within the chimera’s body are considered to be totally digested. The chimera’s body then harmlessly expels indigestible materials (metals, glass, magical materials).

The chimera must have Quicksilver Form active in order to use this power, but she can activate both Knacks reflexively at a cost of seven motes. If Quicksilver Form is already active, there is no mote cost to activate Assimilation of the Prey
