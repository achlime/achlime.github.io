Compendium Lunar Stamina

Halting the Scarlet Flow

Costs: 2m+, Mins: Stamina 4, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, Gift
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Bruise-Relief Method
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 160), Scroll of Errata (p. 55)

Silver light shines from the Lunar’s wounds as lacerations close and bones knit instantly. When the light fades, all that is left are a few bruises. Each use of this Charm heals a single level of lethal damage. This miscellaneous action may be performed as part of a flurry.

When used as a Gift, this Charm requires the commitment of 3m and permits the Lunar to reflexively regenerate one lethal health level at the beginning of each action.
