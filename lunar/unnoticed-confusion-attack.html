Compendium Lunar Charisma

Unnoticed Confusion Attack

Costs: 4m, Mins: Charisma 4, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Social
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Cobra Hypnotic Method
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 166)

This Charm may supplement any social attack, allowing the Exalt’s player to roll (Charisma + relevant Ability) reflexively, and makes anyone whose Mental Defense Value is less than the successes on that roll forget the supplemented social attack. This unnatural influence may still be countered by spending two points of Willpower.
