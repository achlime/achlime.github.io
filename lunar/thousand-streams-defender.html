Thousand Streams Defender

Costs: —, Mins: Charisma 4, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Sacred Guardian Renewal
Source: Glories of the Most High — Luna (p. 25), Scroll of Errata (p. 185)

Luna smiles to see the passion of her Chosen when they rise in defense of the world her lover made. As a permanent upgrade to the Steward’s competence, she doubles the number of dice rolled as a result of channeling any Virtue she has purchased Sacred Guardian Renewal for on any action which protects or promotes anything toward which the Lunar has a positive Intimacy.
