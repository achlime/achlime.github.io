Compendium Lunar Stamina

Frenzied Bear Fortification

Costs: 3m, Mins: Stamina 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 7)
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Armor-Forming Technique or Hide-Toughening Essence
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 160), Scroll of Errata (p. 55)

Luna’s Chosen only become more dangerous as they are wounded, persevering even against the mightiest blows through the sheer force of their outrage. Frenzied Bear Fortification reduces the post-soak damage of an attack against the Lunar by a single die (minimum one). Each point of wound penalty that the Lunar is currently suffering from reduces the damage by an additional die.
