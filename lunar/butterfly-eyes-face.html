Compendium Lunar Manipulation

Butterfly Eyes Face

Costs: 5m, Mins: Manipulation 6, Essence 6, Type: Simple (Speed 3)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Illusion
Duration: (Essence) actions
Prerequisite charms: Butterfly Eyes Fist, One of the Herd
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 73)

Lunars are not who they seem. The target of this Charm suffers an Illusion effect, believing the Lunar to be a nearby person and vice versa unless the target’s reflexive (Perception + Awareness) roll beats a difficulty equal to the Lunar’s Manipulation score. Spending one Willpower point allows the target to tell who is who for one action. After the target has spent three Willpower, the effect ends.
