Compendium Lunar Strength

Unstoppable Lunar Wound

Costs: 1m per die, Mins: Strength 4, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Impressions of Strength
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 145), Scroll of Errata (p. 54)

The might of the Lunar Exalted may not be denied. The Lunar can use this Charm when he damages an opponent. Every two motes spent on this Charm turns a single die of damage into an automatic level of damage in Step 10 of attack resolution. The character may not affect more dice in this way than his Strength.
