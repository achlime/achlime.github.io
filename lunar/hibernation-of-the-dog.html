Compendium Lunar Strength

Hibernation of the Dog

Costs: 6m, Mins: Strength 4, Essence 3, Type: Simple
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Subduing the Honored Foe
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 148)

At times, Lunar Exalted need to ensure that their enemies do not return to full capability too quickly. This Charm is a single attack. If it connects, the target ceases to be able to recover bashing damage from any source for the rest of the scene.
