Roused Bear Throw

Costs: 3m, Mins: Strength 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 10)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Knockback
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Strength Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 147)

Lunar Exalted are masters of close combat. When the character makes a successful clinch attack or manages to control a grapple, he may activate this Charm to throw his opponent up to (Strength x Essence) yards in any direction. This breaks the hold. Targets of this Charm as subtract an external penalty equal to the Lunar’s Essence from their check against knockback.
