Compendium Lunar Dexterity

Pack and Flock Guidance

Costs: —, Mins: Dexterity 4, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Furious Hound Pursuit, Flight of the Sparrow
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 153)

This Charm permanently enhances Furious Hound Pursuit and Flight of the Sparrow, allowing the character to convey the Charm’s benefit to an entire unit that he leads. This adds one Willpower to the cost and affects a unit of up to Magnitude (the character’s Essence - 1). When leading a unit, a Lunar using Furious Hound Pursuit can only target another unit with that Charm’s effects.
