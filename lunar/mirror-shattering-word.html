Mirror-Shattering Word

Costs: —, Mins: Manipulation 6, Essence 5, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Wyld
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Lost Mirror Flight
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 73), Scroll of Errata (p. 174)

Luna rules madness in Creation, and her Chosen act in her name. This Charm permanently enhances Lost Mirror Flight. When using Lost Mirror Flight, the character may double the cost to make the madness permanent. In the Wyld, making insanities permanent costs only one mote extra.
