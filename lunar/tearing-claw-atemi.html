Compendium Lunar Strength

Tearing Claw Atemi

Costs: 3m, Mins: Strength 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Strength Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 145)

This Charm supplements a single attack or a feat of strength that targets or uses an inanimate object. The Charm doubles the attack’s raw damage or doubles the character’s Strength for the purposes of the feat. This applies equally to feats of strength for throwing or breaking objects, even though they normally work on different time scales.
