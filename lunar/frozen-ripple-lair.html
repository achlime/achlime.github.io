Frozen Ripple Lair

Costs: 14m, 2wp, Mins: Wits 6, Essence 7, Type: Simple
Keywords: Obvious, Wyld
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Secure Den Prana, Blinking Eye of Chaos
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 76)

This Charm is identical to Secure Den Prana, but the Lunar declares a time differential for her den in Elsewhere. Time within the redoubt moves faster or slower than it does in Creation by a factor equal to or less than the Lunar’s Essence.
