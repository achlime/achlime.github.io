Compendium Lunar Charisma

Moon Follows Sun Assurance

Costs: —, Mins: Charisma 3, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Sacred Guardian Renewal
Source: Glories of the Most High — Luna (p. 25)

Some Stewards dedicate themselves to the protection of their Solar mate above all other things, discovering an inexhaustible well of resources with which to do so, far beyond those of other Lunar Exalted. Whenever the Lunar would regain Willpower from a stunt that assisted her Solar mate in some way, she may instead choose to restore one die to her Solar Bond pool.
