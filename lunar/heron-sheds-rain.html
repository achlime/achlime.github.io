Heron Sheds Rain

Costs: — (+2m), Mins: Dexterity 4, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Ferocious Guardian Beast Stance, Wary Swallow Method
Source: Glories of the Most High — Luna (p. 23)

This Charm serves as a permanent upgrade to Ferocious Guardian Beast Stance, allowing it to be invoked on the Lunar’s own behalf rather than that of a ward. This adds a two-mote surcharge to the Charm’s invocation cost.
