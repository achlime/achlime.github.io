Butterfly Eyes Enhancement

Costs: —, Mins: Manipulation 8, Essence 7, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Butterfly Eyes Defense or Butterfly Eyes Tread or Butterfly Eyes Fist or Butterfly Eyes Face
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 73)

This Charm permanently enhances all Butterfly Eyes Charms the Lunar knows. Their costs increase by one mote, and the Willpower cost to fend off their mental influence increases by one.
