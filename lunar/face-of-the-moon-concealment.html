Compendium Lunar Charisma

Face of the Moon Concealment

Costs: 2m, Mins: Charisma 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Social, War
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Charisma Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 168)

The Lunar Exalted are masterful guerilla warriors who can lead a unit through thick brush without a trace. This Charm supplements an attempt to conceal a unit for ambush or to disguise a social unit’s intentions until the last moment. Subtract the Lunar’s Essence from the unit’s Magnitude when determining the dice pool for concealment.
