Compendium Lunar Intelligence

Intimacy of the Hunter and the Prey

Costs: 6m, 1wp, Mins: Intelligence 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Righteous Lion Defense
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 188)

Lunars make it their duties and their lives to catch their prey. This Charm prevents a Lunar from betraying the hunt. The player declares a target the character currently attempts to capture or kill. Intimacy of the Hunter and the Prey forms an immediate connection between the Lunar and her target, creating a magically backed Intimacy towards that hunt. She treats mental influence to betray or forsake that hunt as an unacceptable order.

When the hunt ends, the Intimacy fades away. A character may have no more than (Essence) Intimacies empowered by this Charm at one time.
