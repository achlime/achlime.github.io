Compendium Lunar Knacks

Constant Quicksilver Rearrangement

Prerequisites: Lightning-Change Style, Essence 5, Wits 5
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 136)

By committing four motes, the Lunar becomes able to change shape as a completely reflexive action. In combat time, the character may do so no more than once per tick. Lunar Exalted use this in combination with a wide range of forms to evade attacks through small size or soak them with great Stamina. Other uses include rapid evasion of pursuers (even other Lunars) or to bewilder enemies for purposes of confusion, interrogation, torture or other mind games.
