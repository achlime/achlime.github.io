Moonsilver Absorption

Costs: —, Mins: Stamina 6, Essence 5, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Unstoppable Juggernaut Incarnation
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 70)

This Charm permanently enhances Unstoppable Juggernaut Incarnation. When the character uses that Charm, he does not record any of the damage inflicted in the otherwise lethal blow; it now includes aggravated damage. The character’s body shines like moonsilver as it shifts around the blow.
