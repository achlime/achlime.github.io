Cat-Falling Attitude

Costs: 1m, Mins: Dexterity 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: None
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Graceful Crane Stance
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 149)

Height presents no danger to the Stewards. A Lunar may activate this Charm reflexively during or at the end of a fall. Divide the number of yards the character falls by two to determine appropriate damage from falling. The fall does not inflict lethal damage unless the fall covers a distance of at least 50 yards, and the Lunar always lands on her feet—never prone.
