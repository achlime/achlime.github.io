Compendium Lunar Perception

God-Cutting Essence

Costs: 1m, Mins: Perception 4, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Eye of the Cat
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 179)

Luna imbued her Chosen to overthrow the oppressors of Creation. With her supernal perceptive abilities, the Steward knows exactly where to channel her Essence in order to disrupt even an immaterial spirit. This Charm supplements any attack, allowing it to harm immaterial spirits and inflict aggravated damage against them.
