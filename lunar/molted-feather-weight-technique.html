Compendium Lunar Strength

Molted Feather-Weight Technique

Costs: 1m, Mins: Strength 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Tearing Claw Atemi
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 145-146)

Using the power of her great Strength, the Lunar makes an object she attacks with light as a feather to her and just as maneuverable. This Charm negates the accuracy penalty of any weapon, improvised or otherwise, for a single attack.
