Lodestone Reckoning Manner

Costs: 2m, Mins: Wits 2, Essence 1, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 194)

Similar to a swallow flying south, the Lunar knows his way. With this Charm active, the character can never become lost. He has an absolute direction sense, and he can perfectly retrace the path he trod in the last 24 hours. For this Charm to function, the Lunar must have checked his position against the moon sometime within the last 24 hours. Otherwise, the Charm cannot be activated or ends.
