Compendium Lunar Knacks

Devastating Ogre Enhancement

Prerequisites: Deadly Beastman Transformation, Essence 5, Strength 4
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 138)

Taking this Knack increases the size and deadliness of the character’s war form. The hybrid form becomes terrifying in size, dwarfing even the tallest human from at least 12 feet of height. The character also adds one additional dot to each physical Attribute when in the war form, on top of any bonus received from Deadly Beastman Transformation.

Lunars who possess Devastating Ogre Enhancement are widely respected and often feared, even by their peers, for their dedication to the ideal of warfare and their constant honing of their physical form. Most (Lunars, at least) consider them the foremost warriors of Creation.
