Hungry Eagle Method

Costs: 5m, Mins: Wits 4, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 9)
Keywords: Combo-Basic, Counterattack
Duration: Until next action
Prerequisite charms: Furious Unhappy Recourse
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 193)

The overprotective bird pecks anything that comes near her nest or her food. Activating this Charm allows a character to make a counterattack against any person who makes a Melee or Martial Arts attack against her until her next action.
