Silver-Swift Retort

Costs: 2m per, Mins: Wits 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Social
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Meerkat Alertness Practice
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 193)

Stewards save their arguments for the right time. This Charm supplements a social attack, allowing the Lunar to recover more quickly after making her point. Each two motes spent on Silver-Swift Retort reduce the attack’s Speed by 1, to a minimum of 3.
