Compendium Lunar Charisma

Charismatic Lunar Trick

Costs: 6m, Mins: Charisma 4, Essence 4, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Social
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Instinct Memory Insertion
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 167)

Lunars can become extraordinarily persuasive, to the point where their influence becomes difficult to shake off. This Charm infuses Essence into any form of natural mental influence the character attempts, forcing people to consider its validity and turning it into unnatural mental influence. It may be resisted with one point of Willpower, as normal.
