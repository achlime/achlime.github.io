Scorpion and Toad Immunity

Costs: —, Mins: Stamina 4, Essence 3, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Scorpion and Toad Assumption
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 161)

Lunars do not fear what they have conquered. A character with this Charm becomes immune to any poison or disease that he has survived. Once the foreign substance has run its course, it can never harm that character again. Exalted may make exceptions at will: A Lunar does not have to lose the ability to enjoy alcohol, qat or other recreational drugs. Once a character becomes immune, however, she cannot go back.
