Compendium Lunar Intelligence

Righteous Lion Defense

Costs: —, Mins: Intelligence 3, Essence 1, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 188)

This Charm is identical to the Solar Charm Righteous Lion Defense (see Exalted, p. 199) with the following change. Rather than making inviolate an Intimacy to any ideal, the Lunar must choose an Intimacy dedicated to the protection of something, be it a way of life, a place, a society or a specific individual.
