External Hide Perfection

Costs: —, Mins: Stamina 5, Essence 4, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: (Directional) Mastery Technique
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 158)

Lunars become true masters of their territories. This Charm permanently enhances all (Directional) Mastery Technique Charms the character knows. It eliminates the exception made for other characters’ deliberation and intention; the character never suffers damage or penalties from dangers covered by his (Directional) Mastery Technique Charms.
