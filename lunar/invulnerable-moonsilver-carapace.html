Invulnerable Moonsilver Carapace

Costs: — (+3m, 1wp), Mins: Stamina 6, Essence 5, Type: Permanent
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Stone Rhino’s Skin
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 160-161), Scroll of Errata (p. 56)

This Charm upgrades Stone Rhino’s Skin. By paying an additional three motes and a point of Willpower, the Lunar may extend its duration to one action. Moonsilver flows outward from his skin to cover his body in an impervious shell of liquid metal, making this defense Obvious.
