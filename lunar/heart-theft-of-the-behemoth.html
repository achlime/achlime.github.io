Compendium Lunar Knacks

Heart-Theft of the Behemoth

Prerequisites: Green Sun Child, Essence 5, Stamina 5
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 133)

In the First Age, Luna’s mightiest and most clever warriors learned how to hunt down the Primordials’ greatest weapons and steal their shapes and power. This Knack makes even the strangest monster-beasts valid targets for the sacred hunt. Characters with this Knack may take on the shape of primordial creatures that are no larger than a tyrant lizard and rampage to their hearts’ contents. An Essence 7 and Stamina 7 version of the Knack opens the blood hunt to all primordial behemoths.
