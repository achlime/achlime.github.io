Foe-Marking Style

Costs: 2m, Mins: Strength 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Strength Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 142)

In order to avoid killing or crippling each other to prove their points, Lunars of the Silver Pact use this Charm as a method of earning honor from one another. Foe-Marking Style supplements an attempt to inflict a scar on an opponent, either through a called shot or normal lethal damage. Wounds caused by this Charm do not heal normally for the Exalted, leaving scars instead of healing without a blemish. Although this is not a crippling effect, supernatural healing that can cure crippling effects can wipe clean these scars.
