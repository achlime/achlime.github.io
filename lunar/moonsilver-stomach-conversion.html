Compendium Lunar Stamina

Moonsilver Stomach Conversion

Costs: 3m, Mins: Stamina 4, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Gift
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Any Stamina Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 156)

In Creation, almost everything is edible to something. The Lunar Exalted mimic that capability by fortifying their gullets, digestive systems and, occasionally, teeth so they can safely consume nearly anything. Activating the Charm, a character becomes able to eat without harm whatever he puts into his mouth and obtain the same sustenance as from decent food.

Lunar Exalted use this Charm, among other things, to find all the food they need while surviving inhospitable regions of Creation.

When activating Deadly Beastman Transformation, the character may commit one mote to gain the ability to eat materials appropriate to the hybrid form.
