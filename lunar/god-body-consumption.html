Compendium Lunar Perception

God-Body Consumption

Costs: —, Mins: Perception 8, Essence 7, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: God-Body Dissection
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 74), Scroll of Errata (p. 175)

This Charm permanently enhances God-Body Dissection. After successfully severing a spirit’s power with that Charm, the Lunar devours it. It becomes inaccessible to the spirit until the Lunar’s death. This Charm may not permanently devour panoply Charms, such as the signature Charms of the Incarnae.
