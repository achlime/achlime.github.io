Compendium Lunar Dexterity

Lightning Stroke Attack

Costs: 11m, Mins: Dexterity 5, Essence 4, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Twin-Fang Technique, Octopus and Spider Barrage
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 154), Scroll of Errata (p. 54)

Endowing her attack with the speed of her natural grace or the blurring speed of brute force, the character’s attack moves so quickly that both Parry and Dodge Defense Values become inapplicable. Onlookers may think the character and her weapon did not even move. Only a bright flash or blur (and often a falling opponent) indicate that anything happened at all.
