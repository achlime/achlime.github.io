Compendium Lunar Charisma

Snake-Vine Integration

Costs: —, Mins: Charisma 8, Essence 7, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Nature-Reinforcing Allocation
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 71-72), Scroll of Errata (p. 174)

This Charm permanently enhances Nature-Reinforcing Allocation, making plants valid targets. Plants begin with all Abilities and Attributes (except Stamina) at 0 unless they are already capable of mobility, and even their instincts are poor until they have at least Intelligence 1. (On the off chance that someone is playing an uplifted rosebush, the first dot of an Attribute costs 5 xp.)
