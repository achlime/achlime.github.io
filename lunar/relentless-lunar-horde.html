Compendium Lunar Stamina

Relentless Lunar Horde

Costs: —, Mins: Stamina 5, Essence 6, Type: Permanent
Keywords: War
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Relentless Lunar Fury
Source: Dreams of the First Age — Lords of Creation (p. 70)

Relentless Lunar Horde allows the Lunar to use Relentless Lunar Fury while leading a military unit, rousing her warriors into a frenzy. In addition to its standard effects, the unit increases its Close Combat Damage rating by one, increases its Morale by two, does not lose Endurance for the duration and cannot assume close formation. Used in this fashion, Relentless Lunar Fury’s duration is counted in long ticks.
