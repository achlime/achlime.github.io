Compendium Lunar

Any (Attribute) Excellency

Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 140-141)

This prerequisite is satisfied if the Lunar knows any of the alternative Charms listed. The alternative charm must be known for the appropriate Attribute.

Possible alternatives:
  • First (Attribute) Excellency
  • Second (Attribute) Excellency
  • Third (Attribute) Excellency
