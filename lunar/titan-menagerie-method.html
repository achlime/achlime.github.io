Compendium Lunar Knacks

Titan Menagerie Method

Prerequisites: Emperor Ox Expansion, Tyrant Mouse Dominion, Essence 5, Stamina 6
Source: Glories of the Most High — Luna (p. 33)

This Knack permits a Lunar to spend an extra point of Willpower to apply the benefits of Emperor Ox Expansion (see Dreams of the First Age: Book Two—Lords of Creation, p. 67 or The Compass of Terrestrial Directions, Vol. II—The West, p. 144) to any animal form in his Heart’s Blood library.
