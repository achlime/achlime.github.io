Compendium Lunar Stamina

Tiger and Seahorse Dynasty

Costs: —, Mins: Stamina 4, Essence 2, Type: Permanent
Keywords: None
Duration: Permanent
Prerequisite charms: Fish or Fowl Gestation
Source: Glories of the Most High — Luna (p. 24)

This Charm permanently enhances a Lunar’s control over his own biology, and, by extension, his offspring. At the moment of conception he is able to predetermine the gender, Attribute prioritization and one Favored Ability of his offspring. The child is also guaranteed to grow up to be a heroic character. If the Lunar wears a female form, she may also guarantee twins or even triplets.
