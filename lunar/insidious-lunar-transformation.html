Compendium Lunar Knacks

Insidious Lunar Transformation

Prerequisites: Lunar Blood Shaping Technique, Essence 5, Charisma 5
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 133)

This Knack enhances Lunar Blood Shaping Technique. Once the Lunar feeds an amount of his blood to a target unwilling to change, the Lunar and target’s players make opposed (Willpower + Essence) rolls. If the Lunar wins, the target is forced into the chosen form for the normal duration, depending on how much blood she consumed. Otherwise, there is no effect.
