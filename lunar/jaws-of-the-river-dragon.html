Compendium Lunar Strength

Jaws of the River Dragon

Costs: 4m, Mins: Strength 4, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive (Step 10)
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Until next action
Prerequisite charms: Throat-Baring Hold
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 147)

None can escape a Steward’s punishment. In addition to the normal crushing damage that a successful clinch or opposed grapple roll inflicts, the character’s mighty arms also weaken her opponent. The victim of this Charm suffers an external penalty on the next opposed grapple check equal to the Lunar’s Essence.
