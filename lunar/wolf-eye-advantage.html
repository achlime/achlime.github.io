Wolf Eye Advantage

Costs: 1m, Mins: Perception 3, Essence 1, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Any Perception Excellency
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 183)
