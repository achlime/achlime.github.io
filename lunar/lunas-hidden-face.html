Compendium Lunar Knacks

Luna’s Hidden Face

Prerequisites: Essence 3, Perception 4
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Lunars (p. 134)

Lunars have difficulty taking on a shape unnatural to Creation. This Knack makes creatures mutated by the Wyld, even to such an extent that they become unviable bloodlines in Creation, valid targets of the sacred hunt. A Lunar does not gain any of the creature’s Essence-based powers.
