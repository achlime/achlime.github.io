Compendium Martial Arts Live Wire Style

Lightning Supremacy Reversal

Costs: 1m, Mins: Martial Arts 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 1)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Whistling Analog Signal
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 175)

Criminals commonly craft crude weapons for themselves or steal them from their betters. Flashing Wires have found that they are quicker to surrender once relieved of such dubious support. Channeling a quick snap of Essence, the martial artist’s whip flashes through the air to curl around a criminal’s weapon, disarming him in a burst of sparks. When used to supplement a disarm attempt, this Charm converts the standard disarming bonus of the character’s whip into automatic successes and doubles the length the weapon is thrown.
