Greatest Killer Attitude

Costs: 3m, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Bleeding Crescent Strike
Source: Glories of the Most High — Luna (p. 32)

The martial artist’s corona flashes, then surges in one vast rush to the leading edge of an unarmed attack launched at an opponent. An attack enhanced with this Charm adds [3 + (halos x 2)] to its raw damage, or [1 + (halos / 2)] to its post-soak damage, as desired. Invoking this Charm spends all current halos, as in Bleeding Crescent Strike.
