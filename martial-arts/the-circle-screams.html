Compendium Martial Arts Thousand Wounds Gear Style

The Circle Screams

Costs: 1m, Mins: Martial Arts 2, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 171)

The martial artist goes from chambered stillness to blinding action in a heartbeat, unleashing a straight-arm attack with such speed that the air whistles like escaping steam in its wake. This Charm supplements an unarmed martial arts attack, adding either (Essence ÷ 2) Accuracy or (Essence) raw damage to the strike at the martial artist’s discretion.
