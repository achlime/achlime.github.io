Compendium Martial Arts Thousand Wounds Gear Style

Thousand Wounds Persistence

Costs: 5m, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Linear Flight Principle, Hungry Gear-Tooth Attack
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 173)

The martial artist aligns his Essence, his will, his weapon and his target before striking. This Charm supplements a ranged attack with a gyroscopic chakram. As long as the Exalt commits Essence to this Charm and does not voluntarily recall his weapon, the chakram continues to attack its target until the target is dead or the weapon has botched rather than following the usual re-attack rules (see Chapter Six).
