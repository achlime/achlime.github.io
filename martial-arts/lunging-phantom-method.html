Compendium Martial Arts Hungry Ghost Style

Lunging Phantom Method

Costs: 4m, Mins: Martial Arts 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Blood-Scenting Hunger
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 187), Scroll of Errata (p. 88)

The martial artist twists towards and around her foe, elusive as a ghost, positioning herself to strike from behind before her enemy realizes where she’s gone. An unarmed attack supplemented by this Charm ignores cover, and counts 10s as 2 successes on its damage roll.
