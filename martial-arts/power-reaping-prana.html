Compendium Martial Arts Hungry Ghost Style

Power-Reaping Prana

Costs: 4m, Mins: Martial Arts 4, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive (Step 10)
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Hungry Ghost Form
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 187), Scroll of Errata (p. 88)

The Hungry Ghost stylist is nourished by the agony of his enemies, sustaining his fighting spirit with their suffering. The martial artist can use Power-Reaping Prana whenever he deals lethal or aggravated damage to an Essence user with an unarmed attack. He must have already used Blood-Scenting Hunger to assess that target’s Essence pool earlier in the scene. Doing so allows him to regain a single point of Willpower or a channel of Valor. He cannot use it more than once per action.
