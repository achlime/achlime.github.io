Red-Handed Courtesan Style

Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)
Level: Terrestrial

This is a significant rewrite of the original Red-Handed Courtesan Style written by JiveX. The structure

One does not speak of the Red-Handed Courtesans, not in polite company anyway. Their name has been tied to the death of many prestigious figures in courts, not just in the Realm and its Satrapies, but throughout Creation. The Red-Handed Courtesan style knows neither borders, nor elemental aspect, its practitioners come from any class and, despite popular opinion, any gender, they share only a special prowess for the art of sex, and, frequently, are or were, sex workers. It is worth noting, however, that they are not always high paid courtesans, as the name suggests, this style is practiced by streetwalkers and rent boys from nexus as frequently as it is practice by the professional harlots trained at the prestigious Eventide Conservatory in the Imperial City.

Weapons and Armor: Practitioners of this style learn to fight unarmed, and often in the nude (and certainly in very little clothing), they may not wear armor or wield traditional weapons. However, they are adept at turning common bedroom accessories into handy improvised weapons. In the hands of a Red-Handed Courtesan candle is a brutal club, a silk drapery a rope, and a bodice lace a garrote. Storytellers should allow, with a stunt and/or sufficient justification, for items one would normally find in a bedroom to function as improvised versions of mundane martial arts or melee weapons with which they are reasonably analogous. These improvised weapons still have the traits for improvised weapons (Accuracy no greater than -3 and Rate no more than 2) but are considered in form for this style. As an additional rule, storytellers may wish to rule that the Defense trait of such weapons never exceeds +1 and is usually 0.

Complimentary Abilities: Learning this style requires three dots distributed in any manner Between Socialize, Athletics and Performance. Though it is not a requirement to learn these charms, no Sifu would teach a student with an Appearance lower than 3.

Grasping Yoni Prana

Costs: 4m, Mins: Martial Arts 2, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 1)
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

The practiced courtesan has studied well the arts and sciences of pleasing her clients. Her control over the muscles of her lower body is enviable, almost supernatural. This discipline, the Red-Handed Courtesans learned, could easily be applied to combat. The Martial Artist entwines her opponent with her legs, while some may find this position enviable, those who have felt the powerful crushing force the Martial Artist is capable of exerting when her attitude shifts from tenderness to toughness regard the prospect of being so embraced with equal parts fear and desire. Essence grants the Courtesan’s legs greater strength, allowing her to inflict lethal damage with a crushing attack, and adding +2 to the attack’s base damage. The Martial Artist, accustomed to maintaining this grip on bodies in motion, also adds one success to attempts to retain control of or establish this clinch. It is said, though never publicly, that this Charm can be employed, with potentially devastating results, during coitus, though the muscles enhanced in this instance are not the legs. If it supplements a Clinch attack made mid coitus, generally by virtue of a surprise attack, this charm adds +4 to the Clinch attack’s base damage instead of +2.

Undressed By Practiced Hands

Costs: 2m, 1wp or 2m, Mins: Martial Arts 3, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-Basic, Stackable, Touch
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Grasping Yoni Prana
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

The practiced courtesan knows how to free her clients from the confines of their vestments, and can apply this trick to her enemy’s armor. This charm supplements an unarmed Martial Arts attack. The attack suffers a called shot penalty appropiate to the type of armor being targeted; -1 for light armor or clothes, -2 for medium, -3 for heavy and -4 for superheavy armor. The attack does no damage to the target and does not contend with soak, instead the martial artist rolls (Dexterity + Extra Successes) and reduces her target’s Armored soak (both Lethal and Bashing) by 1 point per success. This soak reduction applies against any attack if the armor is mundane, but only against the Martial Artist’s attacks if it is attuned Artifact armor. Armor reduced to zero soak is removed, if it is mundane or unattuned. This charm has no effect on soak provided by charms or sorcery and not resulting from actual physical armament. When used against a target with an intimacy of lust towards the Martial Artist, invocations of this charm which follow a successful application of this charm cost 2m. The Armor remains penalized until the character re-applies it, requiring the normal procedure for donning armor but taking only 60 seconds.

Resilience of Supple Flesh

Costs: 2m, Mins: Martial Arts 2, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 2)
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

Only the most talented of professional lovers are ever taught this style. By the time they learn this martial art, most have satisfied some the appetites of some truly strange and depraved individuals (or in some cases several such individuals at once.) Their skin is still soft supple and firm, it is their business to be beautiful and desirable, but the finely trained muscles beneath give their flesh strength that belies its apparent softness. Blows that should raise welts do not, injuries that should cause bruises merely redden the skin, and cuts are never as deep or as serious as they seem like they should be.

Spending motes to activate this Charm, the character’s Essence augments his soak, reinforcing this impossible toughness. While this charm is active, the Martial Artist adds +(Essence)B/+(Essence/2)L to her natural soak and may Parry Lethal attack’s unarmed. If they are mortal, they also become immune to natural Disfigurement as a result of suffering damage (though they may still suffer from other naturally inflicted Disabling wounds). At Essence 3+ the Courtesan also does not bleed when damage is inflicted upon her. Her Essence maintaining the perfection of her physical body.

Jaw Dropping Revelation

Costs: 2m, Mins: Martial Arts 3, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 1)
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: One Tick
Prerequisite charms: Resilience of Supple Flesh
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

The martial artist activates this Charm when an opponent orients himself to take action. Doing so causes her to ‘accidentally’ bare a glimpse of forbidden flesh to her opponent at a critical moment. This titillating glimpse utterly disrupts the attacker’s concentration, tantalizing provoking lust that compromises the attacker’s ability to perform effectively.

Until the next tick the target suffers a (Martial Artist’s Appearance / 2) internal penalty to all his dice pools (this penalty also affects his DV) until the next tick. This Charm can only be used against opponents that meet at least one of the conditions mentioned in the style’s form charm.

Red Handed Courtesan Form

Costs: 4m, Mins: Martial Arts 4, Essence 2, Type: Simple (Speed 5)
Keywords: Form-type, Combo-Basic, Emotion, Social
Duration: One Scene
Prerequisite charms: Jaw Dropping Revelation, Undressed By Practiced Hands
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

The martial artist’s movements take on the sinuous sexualized quality that is identified with this style. She is unabashed, and unafraid, jaded by her experiences in the world. She could be facing a demonic horde or a single adversary and she looks upon them with the same sultry defiance. Her movements have an exaggerated quality designed to excite, entice or otherwise distract her opponent. For the duration of the combat she allows her sexuality to empower her fighting. She is a being of touch and physicality.

While this Charm is active, anyone targeted by the Martial Artist’s actions suffers a -1 external penalty on DV and MDV for each of the following conditions that is met (these penalties are cumulative):

  • The target bears an intimacy of lust towards the Martial Artist
  • The target was engaged in sex with the Martial Artist in the previous hour
  • The target’s Temperance is exceeded by the Martial Arists’s Appearance

Treat these penalties as a product of an Emotion effect which can be resisted for one action by spending 1wp. This is a form of Unnatural Mental influence. Penalties on DV are only effective for unarmed attacks by the Martial Artist.

Finally, the Martial Artist is so accustomed to bedroom environments she adds an additional +1 to the DV bonus provided by hard cover derived from taking shelter behind furniture, bed-posts, or other household items.

This Form-type charm is not characterized by any aggressively hostile movements and appears as a form of flirtation rather than combat readiness to onlookers.

Bliss-too-Soon Infliction

Costs: 5m, Mins: Martial Arts 4, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 9)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Crippling, Counterattack, Touch, Obvious, Stackable
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Red Handed Courtesan Form
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

Pleasure can be as deadly a weapon as pain in the right hands. The Martial Artist intimate knowledge of the Anatomy and Essence allow her to bring her opponent to mind blanking peaks of Ecstacy. This charm may be invoked in response to any attack made from close range (i.e., all melee attacks without the R tag), the Martial Artist draws near to the target and brings the full force of her understanding of the body’s meridians and command of her essence to bear upon the target. This surge of ecstacy leaves the target gasping and unable to react. Roll (Dexterity + Martial Arts) as an unarmed counterattack against the target, if the attack lands and penetrates hardness, the target suffers the following effects in lieu of Damage (no damage is rolled):

  • They are overwhelmed by orgasmic bliss, all subsequent actions in their current flurry are automatically aborted
  • Exhausted, the target suffers a -1 point fatigue penalty for the Rest of the Scene. This is a Crippling Effect that can be stacked up to (Essence) times.

Fearsome Boudoir Tigress

Costs: 1m or 2m, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 1 or 2)
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Indefinite
Prerequisite charms: Bliss-too-Soon Infliction
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

Just as every object can be a vehicle for the deliverance of pleasure in the hands of the Courtesean, so to can it be a bringer of death. This charm allows the Martial Artist to spend 1m to reflexively ready an improvised weapon which meets the critera established in the Form Weapon’s section above. If doing so would be favorable, and given the nature of improvised weapons it almost always is, the Martial Artist treats the weapon as if it had Speed 5, Accuracy +0, Defense +0 instead of its normal value for those traits. If the martial artist has the Red Handed Courtesan Form active, she may spend 2m when readying this weapon and instead apply the trait bonuses of an Exceptional quality weapon to it. This persists for as long as motes remain commited to the charm. Weapons improvised with this charm are more hardy than they ought to be, but will still break fairly easily. If the character uses his weapon to parry an attack from an attacker with a Strength greater than her (Essence x 2) or which has a base damage greater than her (essence x 3) it becomes useless at the end of the tick and must be discarded and replaced.

Ardent Lover’s Lethal Embrace

Costs: 3m, 1wp, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Illusion
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Bliss-too-Soon Infliction
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

The art of lovemaking goes hand in hand with the art of murder for the Red-Handed Courtesans. They practice both with the same prowess and ecstatic abandon. Thus, none who lie with a Courtesan can truly know whether her she will bring them bliss or death. Indeed, she often will do both. This charm supplements an unarmed Martial Arts attack against a character the Martial Artist is currently interacting with socially. It explicitly allows this attack to resolve as if it were a surprise attack without requiring the martial artist to take any Stealth actions, the magic of the charm compensates.

The Martial Artist’s (Manipulation + Martial Arts) against the target’s MDV. The target’s MDV is modified by the relative Appearance modifier as if this was a social attack. If the target is engaged in coitus with the martial artist, they are considered distracted imposing another -1 external penalty on their MDV. If this roll succeeds, the Martial Artist hides her killing intent beneath a veil of unbridled eroticism, rendering the attack unexpected.

This charm is a unnatural mental influence Illusion effect, and the target may spend 2wp to negate the attack’s unexpected aspect. Targets subject to this attack seldom let their guard down around the Martial Artist, becoming immune to it, regardless of its success or failure, for their (Temperance) Weeks thereafter.

Passion’s Aria Masquerade

Costs: 4m, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK, Illusion, Emotion
Duration: One Scene
Prerequisite charms: Ardent Lover’s Lethal Embrace, Fearsome Boudoir Tigress
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

The Martial Artist can blur the line between exstacy and agony, such that to outsiders it is impossible to tell which the Martial Artist is inflicting.

The Martial Artist rolls (Manipulation + Martial Arts) against the MDV of any who witness her. The target’s MDV is modified by the relative Appearance modifier as if this was a social attack. If succesful, the target cannot tell that the Martial Artist and whomever she is fighting are engaged in a bout rather than a form of sexual gratification. The blows of her enemies are merely the actions of a passionate lover in the eyes of onlookers, and screams and wails merely the sounds of passion to thier ears.

Characters who cannot directly see the Martial Artist or her partner(s) must pay 2wp too look through this illusion, while characters who can have an easier time seeing the truth, and may pay 1wp. Any mortal affected by this charm counts a scene spent under its effects as a scene spent building an intimacy of lust towards the Martial Artist. This is an Emotion effect which cannot be resisted without resisting the Illusion effect, which dismisses it automatically. The mental influence imposed by this charm is unnatural mental influence. Characters engaged in combat with the martial artist or are unnafected.

Unrelenting Lover’s Exertion

Costs: 4m, 1wp, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive (Step 1)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Crippling, Illusion, Emotion
Duration: One Clinch
Prerequisite charms: Passion’s Aria Masquerade
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

The Martial Artist is a proficient but physically demanding lover, driving her target to degrees of exertion that are as taxing as they are rewarding. The Martial Artist readily applies this principle in combat. This charm benefits a Hold action taken while in control of a Clinch. Overwhelmed with pleasure, the target rolls (Stamina + Resistance) against a difficulty of the Martial Artist’s (Martial Arts) score. Failure imposes -1 point of fatigue penalty as a Crippling effect. As long as the Martial Artist retains control of the clinch, and does not Crush the target, but rather Holds them, they must make this check each time they loose the contested roll for control of the clinch.

If the target’s Fatigue Penalties rise beyond their (Stamina + Resistance) they immediately fall unconscious and the Martial Artist rolls (Manipulation + Martial Arts) against the target’s MDV. The target’s MDV is modified by the relative Appearance modifier as if this was a social attack. If this roll succeeds, the target awakes with an intimacy of Lust towards the Martial Artist. This is an Emotion effect that can be resisted for 2wp or 1wp if the character is not attracted to the Martial Artist’s Gender or Species.

Furthermore, if the Clinch was an unexpected attack, they are subject to an Illusion effect that causes them to remember their encounter with the Martial Artist as a passionate and vigorous love-making session rather than a martial engagement. This illusion effect cannot be resisted without resisting the Emotion effect and if the Emotion effect is resisted the Illusion evaporates as well. This charm exerts unnatural mental influence.

Courtesan’s Pernicious Crescendo

Costs: 4m, 1wp, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 3, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Illusion
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Passion’s Aria Masquerade
Source: Custom (Brend Wanders), Custom (Niels van Tol)

This is the most feared technique of the Red Handed Courtesan Style. Many are the stories of courtesans who used this technique on their unwitting companions while in the throes of passion, and keeping them there until they expired. This Charm supplements an unarmed Martial Arts attack. The attack, should it land, does lethal damage. The Courtesan’s hand is reinforced by Essence, taking on the properties of a blade. Should the attack do more than one level of lethal damage, the courtesan may opt to reduce the damage to one level and instead make the wound bleed profusely. Delaying her gratification, the Courtesan twists and flexes her fingers inside the wound, causing it to bleed and become more difficult to treat.

Mortals attempting to close the wound find the difficulty of the (Wits + Medicine) rises by the martial artist’s Essence. Furthermore the wound bleeds more rapidly, they loose one level of lethal damage every (Stamina) ticks, as the blood hemorrhages rapidly from their body. Exalts and Essence-users fare somewhat better, the difficulty of the roll to reflexively close the wound does not rise. However, they do bleed out at an increased rate, suffering one lethal level every (Stamina) actions. The treatment of any being with Medicine 2 or higher automatically closes these wounds.

Furthermore, if the attack was an unexpected attack the Martial Artist rolls (Manipulation + Martial Arts) against the target’s MDV. The target’s MDV is modified by the relative Appearance modifier as if this was a social attack. If this roll succeeds, the target is unaware that he is wounded so, and instead climaxes violently each time he loses a health level. This imposes a -2 fatigue penalty while the wound is open. This is an Illusion effect that can be resisted with 2wp or 1wp if the character is not attracted to the Martial Artist’s Gender or Species. This Charm exerts unnatural mental influence.
