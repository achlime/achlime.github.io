Compendium Martial Arts Snake Style

Essence Venom Strike

Costs: 7m, 1wp, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 4, Type: Simple (Speed 4, DV -1)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Armor-Penetrating Fang Strike, Striking Serpent Speed
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 242)

The master of Snake Style is quick as lightning and as deadly as a doze serpents. The Exalt makes an unarmed Martial Arts attack. This Charm makes the attack’s damage aggravated. It also adds the character’s permanent Essence score to the damage of the attack.
