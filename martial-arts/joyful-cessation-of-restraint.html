Compendium Martial Arts Infernal Monster Style

Joyful Cessation of Restraint

Costs: 3m, 1wp, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 3, Type: Extra Action
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Glory to the Demon-Monster
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Infernals (p. 160)

The martial artist allows the way of the Infernal Monster to possess him utterly, quickening his movements into a blur of ultraviolence. This Charm is a magical flurry of (Essence) unarmed attacks against a single target. These attacks ignore rate limitations, suffer no multiple action penalties and impose a DV penalty equal to the highest penalty for any one attack. If the target is an animate being who is prone as of sequentially resolving the final attack in this cascade, the Infernal can make two additional attacks as part of the flurry, and these final attacks can’t be dodged.
