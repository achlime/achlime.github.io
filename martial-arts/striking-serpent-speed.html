Compendium Martial Arts Snake Style

Striking Serpent Speed

Costs: 6m, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 2, Type: Extra Action
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Uncoiling Serpent Prana
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 242)

Practicioners of Snake Style martial arts must internalize the raw speed and reflexes of a coiled snake. The player rolls (the Exalt’s Essence × 2). This Charm is a magical flurry containing one action per success. These actions do not need to be attacks, but the character cannot take actions that would require more than seven ticks performed on their own.
