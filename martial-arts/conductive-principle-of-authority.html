Compendium Martial Arts Live Wire Style

Conductive Principle of Authority

Costs: 3m, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 2, Type: Reflexive (Step 1)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Live Wire Form
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 176)

Sometimes, offenders refuse to recognize the primacy of law, even once found out and ensnared. The Flashing Wires shrug; those that will not kneel will be knelt. The martial artist concentrates his Essence around his heart, then releases it down his arm and through his whip in one vast, brutal surge. This Charm can boost crushing damage with a clinch using a whip, adding the attacker’s Martial Arts rating to his raw damage. Alternatively, the Charm can enhance a whip strike, making the attack’s damage piercing.
