Compendium Martial Arts Sapphire Veil of Passion Style

Someone Else’s Consequence

Costs: 15m, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 5, Type: Reflexive (Step 10)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious, Shaping
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Sapphire Veil of Passion Form
Source: Glories of the Most High — the Maidens of Destiny (p. 30)

The Elder Sutra of Desire: One morning, the maiden awoke…

Advanced students of the Sapphire Veil of Passion are among Creation’s more careless fighters (and lovers), unconcerned with the consequences of their actions. This Charm may be activated when the martial artist successfully strikes an opponent with an unarmed attack, levying one of the following effects:

  • The martial artist immediately terminates the effects of all unnatural mental influence from which she suffers, transferring them to her target, who must then deal with them as he is best able.
  • The martial artist immediately terminates the effects of all Poison and Sickness effects from which she suffers, transferring them to her target.
