Compendium Martial Arts White Reaper Style

Flickering Corona Barrier

Costs: 5m, 1wp, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive (Step 2)
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: One scene
Prerequisite charms: Impenetrable White Shroud
Source: Glories of the Most High — Luna (p. 32)

Executing a lightning-fast kata, the White Reaper practitioner bends the arcing nimbus of power around herself into a flickering shield. For the duration of this Charm, the martial artist adds (1 + current number of halos) to her Parry DV. This value is not static, but fluctuates as the character gains and spends halos throughout the scene.
