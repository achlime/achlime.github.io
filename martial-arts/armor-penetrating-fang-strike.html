Compendium Martial Arts Snake Style

Armor-Penetrating Fang Strike

Costs: 4m, 1wp, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Essence Fangs and Scales Technique
Source: Core Rulebook (p. 240-241)

The nix-snakes of the East can bit through tempered steel. The students of Snake Style lean similar ferocity. This Charm enhances an unarmed Martial Arts attack. It allows the attack to ignore the target’s soak from armor. The target can only soak with Stamina and Charms such as Iron Skin Concentration.
