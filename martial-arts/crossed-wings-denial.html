Compendium Martial Arts Crane Style

Crossed Wings Denial

Costs: 6m+, Mins: Martial Arts 5, Essence 3, Type: Reflexive (Step 2)
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: Crane Form
Source: Glories of the Most High — the Maidens of Destiny (p. 23), Scroll of Errata (p. 186)

What monster kills so beautiful a bird? This Charm may be activated in response to any physical attack of which the martial artist is aware, allowing her to make a perfect unarmed parry even against unblockable attacks. Doing so has a base cost of six motes, plus an additional mote for each time the martial artist has activated Crossed Wings Denial previously in the scene. Use of this Charm prevents the martial artist from making any physical attacks on her next action as a special flaw of invulnerability. Counterattacks are not similarly prohibited.
