Ravaging Blow

Costs: 2m, Mins: Martial Arts 2, Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: Combo-OK, Enhanced
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Abyssals (p. 183), Scroll of Errata (p. 87)

Abyssal martial artists strike where it hurts most. This Charm enhances an unarmed attack (other than a grapple). If the attack roll succeeds, extra successes count double for determining raw damage. If the Exalt has Dark Messiah Form active, Ravaging Blow also adds one to the attack’s minimum damage.

Enhanced: While Dark Messiah Form is active, Ravaging Blow also grants the Abyssal’s natural attacks the magical material benefits of soulsteel.
