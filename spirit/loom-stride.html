Compendium Spirit Relocations

Loom Stride

Costs: 4m, Mins: Essence 2, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: The Books of Sorcery, Vol. IV — the Roll of Glorious Divinity I (p. 157)

The spirit replaces its normal Move action with a special Move action that doesn’t require it to cross space. This Charm must be invoked on a tick on which the spirit acts. The spirit focuses on a point within (Essence x Conviction) yards, and its player rolls (Wits + Conviction), taking into account vision and cover-based penalties as though the spirit were making a ranged attack. With a successful roll, the spirit instantaneously moves up to (Essence x Conviction) yards. The spirit cannot dash or reflexively move until its next action. Successes on this roll constitute successes on a roll for the spirit to reestablish surprise, which is resisted normally.
