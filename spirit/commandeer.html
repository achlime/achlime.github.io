Compendium Spirit Inhabitings

Commandeer

Costs: 2m, Mins: Essence 5, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: The Books of Sorcery, Vol. IV — the Roll of Glorious Divinity I (p. 160)

This Charm has two effects. If the spirit touches a host and a successful roll of the spirit’s (Essence + Temperance) is made against an external penalty of a possessing spirit’s ([Essence + Temperance] / 2), it may eject the current inhabitant. Second, if this Charm is used while another character is attempting to eject or exercise the spirit from its host, it may activate this Charm to add an external penalty of (spirit’s Temperance) to any rolls to do so. Any character who is successfully rebuked through either effect of this Charm by a spirit with a higher Essence than her own loses a number of Willpower points, is knocked back a number of yards and is considered Inactive for a number of actions equal to the difference in Essence scores.
