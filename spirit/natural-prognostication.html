Compendium Spirit Divinations

Natural Prognostication

Costs: 1m, Mins: Essence 1, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: None
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: The Books of Sorcery, Vol. IV — the Roll of Glorious Divinity I (p. 149)

The spirit’s words and actions casually reveal fate’s plans. This Charm activates automatically when the spirit encounters a character or region for which Heaven has a weighty plan. The Charm reveals the preplanned fates of those with the Destiny Background (see Scroll of the Monk, p. 151), though the Charm cannot determine the Background’s strength. Natural Prognostication also senses the effects of ascending or descending destinies caused by Sidereal astrology.
