Compendium Spirit Autochtonian

Diagnostics

Costs: 2m, Mins: Essence 2, Type: Supplemental
Keywords: None
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 76)

By activating this Charm, the subroutine can instantly diagnose the source of any malfunction that hinders the operation of a machine, an automaton or a machine spirit. After using this Charm, add the subroutine’s Essence to any Craft rolls made to effect appropriate repairs.
