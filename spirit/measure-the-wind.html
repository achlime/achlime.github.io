Compendium Spirit General

Measure the Wind

Costs: 1m, Mins: Essence 1, Type: Reflexive
Keywords: Combo-OK
Duration: Instant
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: The Books of Sorcery, Vol. IV — the Roll of Glorious Divinity I (p. 143-144)

This Charm determines the Essence trait of all living creatures within a number of yards equal to the spirit’s Willpower. This Charm also allows for a (Wits + Perception) roll to find hidden creatures within this range, and the difficulty of this roll is reduced by 2. This Charm recognizes what type of being each creature is—Exalt, spirit, ghost, etc. Finally, the Charm detects whether any creature the spirit examines is capable of killing a god permanently, as with Ghost-Eating Technique.

Measure the Wind follows all Divinations guidelines (see p. 148).
