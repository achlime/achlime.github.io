Inurement

Costs: 2m, Mins: Essence 4, Type: Simple
Keywords: Obvious
Duration: One day
Prerequisite charms: None
Source: Manual of Exalted Power — The Alchemicals (p. 76)

This Charm allows a subroutine to ignore the effects of a hostile environment. While the effects last, the subroutine enjoys the benefit of three submodules from the Alchemical Charm Industrial Survival Frame (see pp. 137-138), selected at the time of the Charm’s purchase.
